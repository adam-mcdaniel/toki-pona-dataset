{{nimi ijo|'''jan Mosata'''|Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart}} li jan sitelen suli pi [[kalama musi Kelasise]]. jan mute li toki e ni: kalama musi pi jan Mosata li pona mute a.
jan Mosata li [[jan lili]] la, ona li open e pana pi kalama musi. tenpo lon ona la, jan mute li kama sona e ona. taso, ona li jo e mani lili. tenpo lon ona li lili, taso ona li pana e sitelen kalama mute mute.
pali pi jan Mosata li pana mute tawa jan ante pi kalama musi. [[jan Petopen]] li kama jo e ijo lawa mute tan pali pi jan Mosata.<ref>Jahn, Otto; Townsend, Pauline D.; Grove, George (1882). [https://archive.org/details/lifeofmozart03jahn Life of Mozart]. London, Novello, Ewer & Co.</ref>
