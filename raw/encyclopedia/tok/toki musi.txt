==sitelen pi toki musi==
[[sitelen:Calligramme.jpg|thumb|150px|toki musi ni li pali e sitelen pi lawa meli]]
{{nimi ijo|'''sitelen pi toki musi'''|literature}} li toki musi lon [[sitelen toki]]. ken la, sitelen li nasin pi awen toki musi. kulupu jan pi sitelen toki la, sitelen pi toki musi li kama suli mute tawa [[nasin jan]]. ona li suli tawa musi tawa [[sona]] tawa [[nasin sewi]] tawa [[nasin lawa]]. ona li nasin mute pi sitelen pi toki musi. nasin suli pi sitelen musi li [[sitelen pi kalama musi]]<!--poetry--> li [[sitelen toki pi pali musi]]<!--drama anu stagewriting--> li [[sitelen Posa]]<!--prose--> (sitelen Posa li nasin ali ante pi sitelen toki musi).
tenpo pini mute la, jan mute li kepeken kiwen tawa sitelen e toki musi. tenpo ni la, jan mute li kepeken [[lipu toki]] anu [[ilo sona]] tawa sitelen e toki musi.
