{{nimi ijo|'''jan Wankelo Otese Papatanusiju'''|  , Evngelos Odyssas Papathanassou|toki Elena|ipa=[evaelos oiseas papaanasi.u]}} anu {{nimi ijo|'''jan Wankeli'''|Vangelis|ipa=[vaelis]}} li jan pi kalama musi tan [[ma Elena]]. ona li pana e [[kalama musi pi wawa linja]] e [[kalama musi Kelasike]] e kalama musi Pokesi <!--progressive rock-->.
jan mute li sona e ona tan ni: ona li pali e kalama musi pi [[sitelen tawa]] mute. ni mute li {{nimi ijo|[[sitelen tawa "ilo tawa soweli seli"]]|Chariots of Fire|toki Inli}} li {{nimi ijo|[[sitelen tawa Pelejewana]]|Blade Runner}} li {{nimi ijo|[[sitelen tawa "tenpo sike 1492: lanpan pi ma pona"]]|1492: Conquest of Paradise}} li {{nimi ijo|[[sitelen tawa "mun: tawa pi jan wan"]]|Cosmos: A Personal Voyage}}.
taso, tenpo open pi pali ona la, ona li lon kulupu mute pi kalama musi. tenpo sike nanpa 1968, ona li kama lon e {{nimi ijo|[[kulupu Aposite Sije]]|Aphrodite's Child|toki Inli|2b=jan lili pi mama sewi Aposite|3b=toki pona}}. tenpo sike 1972 la, kulupu ni li pana e kulupu pi sitelen kalama "666". sitelen kalama ni li pona mute tawa jan mute li pana e ijo mute tawa kalama musi ante mute. tenpo ni la, kulupu ni li pini. jan Wankeli li pali e kalama musi ante mute kin. ona li pali poka e [[kulupu NASA]] e [[kulupu ESA]] tan ni: ona li pana kalama musi pi tawa e [[sewi pimeja]].
ona li kama moli lon tenpo sike nanpa 2022.
