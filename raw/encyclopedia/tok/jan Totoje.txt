{{nimi ijo|'''jan Lepe Totoje'''| |toki Losi}} li jan pi sitelen toki suli tan [[ma Losi]]. jan mute li sona ona tan sitelen ona ni: [[lipu "utala en pona"]] (tenpo 1869) en [[lipu Ana Kajenina]] (tenpo 1878).<ref>[https://www.britannica.com/biography/Leo-Tolstoy Leo Tolstoy | Russian writer | Brittanica]</ref>
