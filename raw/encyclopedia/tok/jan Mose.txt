{{nimi ijo|'''jan Mose'''||toki Iwisi pi tenpo pini|jan li kama jo e ijo tan telo|ipa=}} li jan sewi. ona li [[jan sona]] pi [[nasin sewi Juta]]. ona li kama e [[tawa pi jan Jejuta tan ma Masu]]. ona li jan suli lon nasin sewi Juta. nimi ona ante li '''' ([[toki Alapi]]), li ''M'' ([[toki Elina]]), li ''Moyses'' ([[toki Lasina]]).[[File:The Finding of Moses.jpg|thumb|jan lili pi jan lawa e open e jan Mose|alt=|left|185x185px]]
== lon ==
lon ona li lon [[lipu Pentateko]]. ona li [[jan Lewi]]. ona li kama sin lon [[ma Masu]] pi tenpo pini. [[jan lawa]] pi ma Masu li toki e ni: o pana e jan lili Jejuta tawa [[nasin telo Nile]]. [[jan Jokawete]] (mama meli pi jan Mose) li pali ala e ni. ona li pana e ona lon [[ma lon poka telo]] pi nasin telo Nile. jan lili meli pi jan lawa li kama jo e ona. ona li pana e ona tawa jan Jokawete ona. jan lili pi jan lawa li nimi e ona "Mose" (jan kama jo tan telo). jan Mose kama tawa jan lili pi jan lawa.[[File:Aivazovsky Passage of the Jews through the Red Sea.jpg|thumb|jan li kama lon [[telo suli loje]]|alt=|left|223x223px]]jan Mose li moli e jan Jute pi ma Masu. ni li kama tawa jan lawa. jan Mose li kama tan ma Masu. ona li kama tawa [[jan Jopo|jan sewi Jopo]]. jan Mose li olin e [[jan Sepola]] (jan lili meli pi jan sewi). ona mute li pali e jan lili: [[jan Kilsan]] en [[jan Elijese]].
jan [[sewi Akwe]] li toki tawa ona e ni: "o open e kulupu jan sina". jan sewi li pana e palisa tawa ona. jan Mose en [[jan Alon]] ([[jan pi mama sama|mije sama ona]]) li kama tawa jan lawa. ona li toki e ni: "o open e [[kulupu jan Jejuta]]". jan lawa li pali ala e ni. [[ike luka luka]] li lon ma Masu: [[telo sijelo loje|telo loje]] lon [[nasin telo Nile]]; [[akesi]] mute; [[pipi]] mute tu wan; [[soweli]] li moli; [[jaki lili]] lon jan en soweli; [[lete]] en [[seli]] tan sewi; pimeja ali; jan lili pi jan en soweli li moli. jan lawa li open e kulupu jan Jejuta tan ni.
jan sewi li lawa e ona e kulupu jan Jejuta. jan sewi li tu e [[telo suli loje]]. jan li kama tawa [[ma lon poka telo]] ante pi telo suli.  [[jan utala]] pi jan lawa pi ma Masi li kama tawa ona mute. taso ona li moli. jan Mose li lawa e jan. ona li kama tawa [[ma Isale]]. ona li lon poka ma Isale la, jan sewi li toki tawa jan Mose e ni: "o tawa kama [[kulupu nena Alawin]]". jan Mose li kama tawa [[kulupu nena Nepo]] en moli.
