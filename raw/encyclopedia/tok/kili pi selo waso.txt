'''kili pi selo waso''' li [[kili]] pi {{nimi ijo|[[kasi Akinita pi ma Sonko]]|Actinidia chinensis|toki Lasina||ipa=}} en {{nimi ijo|[[kasi Akinita Telikosa]]|Actinidia deliciosa|toki Lasina||ipa=}}. ona li tan [[ma Sonko]]. ni la jan li nimi "kasi pi ma Sonko" e ona. ona li jelo laso lon insa. kili Kiwi jelo li jelo lon insa.
jan pi ma Nusilan li kepeken nimi {{nimi ijo|"kiwi"|huakiwi|toki Mawi||ipa=}} tan ni: lukin la ona li sama [[waso Kiwi|waso pi nimi "kiwi"]].
[[sitelen:Kiwifruit_(Chinese_characters).svg|alt=|thumb|212x212px|nimi kili lon [[toki Sonko]] (lon sewi) lon [[toki Sonko pona]] (lon anpa)]]
==tenpo pini==
jan li sitelen e kili lon tenpo pini mute. kili ni li kama tawa [[ma Nusilan]]. kili ni li pona tawa jan utala tan [[ma Juke]] tan [[ma Mewika]] lon [[utala suli nanpa tu]]. kili ni li kama tawa ma Juke tawa [[ma Kaliponja]]. kili li lon ma seli lon tenpo ni.
==kili jelo pi selo waso==
[[sitelen:Golden_kiwi.jpg|alt=|left|thumb|182x182px|sitelen pi kili Kiwi jelo]]
{{nimi ijo|kili jelo pi selo waso|Kiwi Gold|toki Inli||ipa=}} li kili pi kasi Akinita pi ma Sonko. tenpo sike nanpa 1992 la, kulupu ''Zespri International Ltd.'' li pali e ona. tenpo sike nanpa 1998 la, ona li lon esun pi [[ma Nijon]]. tenpo sike nanpa 2000 la, ona li lon esun pi [[ma Elopa]].
kili jelo li suwi mute. kili ante li suwi lili.
