'''lipu sona TaNaKa''' li lipu sona pi [[nasin sewi Juta]].
lipu ni li insa kin lipu sona pi [[nasin sewi Kolisu]].
lon lipu ni li lawa mute li toki mute lon jan mute.
lipu sona TaNaKa li lon [[toki Iwisi]] pi tenpo pini pini.
nimi TaNaKa li lon [[toki Iwisi]] la li " ([[SAMPA]]: tanax).
lon lipu sona TaNaKa li lipu sona ni:
*lipu sona Tora ( - toRa) - toki sona
*lipu sona Nepim ( - nevi?im) - jan mute li ken sona e tenpo kama
*lipu sona Katuwim ( - ktuvim) - sitelen toki
lipu sona ni li suli tawa jan mute. jan mute li tawa e lipu sona ni lon [[toki]] mute.
lawa pi lipu sona TaNaKa li ni:
lon [[toki Iwisi]]: lon [[sitelen nimi Iwisi]] en lon [[SAMPA]]:
<pre>
(1)       
/beReSit baRa elohim et haSamajim ve?et ha?aRets/
(2)              
/veha?aRets hajta tohu vavohu veXoSex ?\al pnej tehom veRuaX elohim meRaXefet ?\al pnej hamajim/
(3)      
/vajomeR elohim jehi oR vajehi oR/
</pre>
lon toki pona:
<pre>
(1) tenpo open la sewi Jawe li pali e kon sewi e ma.
(2) ma li pimeja pi insa ala. kon pi sewi Jawe li tawa lon sewi telo.
(3) sewi Jawe li toki e ni: "suno o lon." suno li lon.
</pre>
