{{nimi ijo|'''ilo pi sitelen weka'''|television|toki Inli}} li ken pana e [[sitelen tawa]] kepeken kon. ken la, sitelen tawa ni li jo e walo en pimeja taso anu kule ali. ona li pana e kalama kin. jan li kepeken e ilo pi sitelen weka tawa pana e [[toki pi kama sin]] e [[musi]] e [[sona]].
ilo pi kalama weka la, ilo pi sitelen weka kepeken e [[tawa nena]] sama e ilo sama mute. tenpo open la, ilo pi sitelen weka li kama lon e kulupu pi tenpo sike 1920s. taso, jan nanpa lili li jo e ilo ni lon tenpo open. ilo ni li tawa e jan mute lon tenpo 1950s. tenpo open la, sitelen weka li lon pimeja lon walo taso. taso ilo li kama ante sin la, jan li open e pana pi sitelen kule. ilo pi sitelen weka li kama suli mute tawa [[nasin jan]].
