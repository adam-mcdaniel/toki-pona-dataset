[[File:Linja resized.gif|thumb|sitelen linja]]
linja li suli sewi li lili poka la linja en [[palisa]] li sama. linja li ken ante e nasin ona la linja en palisa li ante.
==linja pi selo jan==
jan li ken jo e kulelinja pi ante ni:
*linja pi kule walo
*linja pi kule pimeja
*linja pi kule loje
*linja pi kule loje pimeja
*linja pi kule insa pi pimeja en insa
*linja pi kule walo walo
kin la jan li ken ante e kule ona kepeken kule.
==linja pi [[ilo kalama]]==
[[File:DuplexScaling.jpg|thumb|left|188px|linja ni li lon ilo kalama]]linja pi ilo kalama li lon ilo kalama tan ni: kepeken e ona la kalama li kama lon.
==linja pi pana e telo==
[[File:linja_pi_pana_e_telo.jpg|thumb|190px|linja pi pana e telo|link=Special:FilePath/linja_pi_pana_e_telo.jpg]]linja ni li ken pana e telo tawa tomo sina, tawa tomokasi sina.
==linja kiwen==
[[File:linja_kiwen.jpg|thumb|left|184px|linja kiwen|link=Special:FilePath/linja_kiwen.jpg]]linja kiwen li ni: sike lili pi mute li kamawan li kama linja. ona li kiwen.
==linja wawa==
[[File:linja_wawa.jpg|thumb|146px|linja wawa|link=Special:FilePath/linja_wawa.jpg]]linja wawa li ken pana e wawa tawa ilo sona sina.
==linja len==
[[File:SpoolsOfThread.jpg|thumb|left|204px|palisa awen pi linja li lon mute]]linja len limute lon len. jan li ken pali e len kepeken linja ni.
