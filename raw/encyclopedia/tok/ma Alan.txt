{{nimi ijo|'''ma Alan'''|ire|toki Alan|2b=Ireland|3b=toki Inli}} li ma lon [[ma Elopa]]. ona li lon [[ma sike telo Alan]], li lon poka pi [[ma Alan lete]] ([[ma Juke]]).
== tenpo pini ==
ma Alan pi tenpo ni li open lon tenpo sike nanpa 1921.
[[ma Inli]] li lawa e ma sike telo Alan lon sike suno ale nanpa 12. jan Alan li wile e ni: jan Inli li awen ala lon ma Alan. tan ni la, kulupu nasin pi lawa jan li lon lon tenpo mute. tenpo sike nanpa 1798 la, kulupu nasin pi lawa jan li utala e jan Inli tan ni: ona li wile kama jo e ma. taso ni li lon ala. tenpo sike nanpa 1801 la, [[ma Piten]] en ma Alan li wan tawa [[ma Juke]].
tenpo sike kama lili pi tenpo sike nanpa 1845 la, [[kili pan ma]] li lili tan ijo jaki. jan mute li moli tan ni: ona ken moku ala. jan mute li tawa [[ma Mewika]] kin.
tenpo sike nanpa 1919 la kulupu nasin pi lawa jan li utala e jan Inli. ona li kama jo e ma mute. tomo lawa Alan en tomo lawa Piten li wile e ni: ma Alan li ken e pali wile taso [[ma Alan lete]] li ken awen e ma lili pi ma Juke.
