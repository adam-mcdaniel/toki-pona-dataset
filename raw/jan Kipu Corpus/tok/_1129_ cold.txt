    The cold wind and the sun.

The cold wind and the sun argued about which of them was the stronger, which the weaker.  .  
A  man came along.  He had a warm coat.
The cold wind and the sun agreed that, if one could get rid of the coat, he was indeed the stronger.  
The cold wind blew strongly.  But he made the man cold and wanting to keep his coat.  The cold wind could not get rid of the coat.  
The sun came ahd heated powerfully.  This time the man discarded the coat.
The cold wind said "I am less strong, the sun is stronger."
