        [Petruccho's mother can know Ezhi's father. Ezhi's father is strong.  Ezhi is weak.  Petruccho also is weak.  But Ezhi is extra weak.  The mother is extra strong.  She is capable of everything and does all these: fetches water, washes the clothes, makes the socks, goes to the food garden, watches many animals,  builds the house, destroys the burning plants, fights Petruccho and his father and Ezhi's father and Ezezhi and Petruccho and many animals.
        Petruccho's mother is strong but she is lazy.  Events come to this: she is asleep now and wakes up at dawn.  Every day is like this.]
