Joshua Birns-Sprague
tenpo suno pini la mi tawa lon sewi pi telo suli kepeken poki suli. poki suli li ken jo e tomo tawa pi mute suli. poki suli li wawa la tawa mi li pona lon tenpo ale. taso tawa mi li ike lon tenpo suno pini. kon li wawa lon telo suli. poki suli li tawa poka wan li tawa poka ante. tenpo wan la poki suli li tawa mute. ijo mute li tawa noka li kalama. jan ale li lukin e sama li pilin pi wawa ala. mi pilin e ni: poki suli li ken tawa noka pi telo suli. mi en meli mi en jan lili mi li ken kama moli. taso mi ale li moli ala. ma li pona!
John Clifford 
toki pona! taso ken la 'tawa anpa pi telo suli'? 
Joshua Birns-Sprague 
ken. mi pilin e ni: ma li lon anpa pi telo suli. poki suli li pakala la ona li ken kama lon noka pi telo suli li ken ala kama lon anpa pi telo suli. taso ona li ken tawa anpa li kama ala lon anpa.

John Clifford

Joshua Birns-Sprague
Yesterday I went on [not sure why `sewi'] the ocean in a ship.  The ship can hold hugely many cars.  If the ship is powerful, my trip is always good. But my trip was bad yesterday.  The winds were strong on the ocean.  The ship went with one, and went with another.  Once the ship moved a lot.  Many things fell and made noises.  Everybody saw the same things and felt weak.  I thought that the ship could go down in the ocean.  Me and my wife and children could die.  Be we didn't die.  Ground is good.  
John Clifford 
Nice tale.  But maybe "go to the bottom of the ocean"
Joshua Birns-Sprague 
Possibly.  I think that there is ground at the bottom of the ocean.  If a boat is destroyed, it can come to the bottom of the ocean but cannot come to the underneath of the ocean.  But it can come toward the underneath of the ocean and not come to the underneath.

John Clifford 
Good point.  This is hard.  People say the front truth of things.]
