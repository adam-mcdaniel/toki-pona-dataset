17a
tenpo sike 2009 (Tu Ka Ka Lupo) pona!  (tu pipi en lupo)
o jan ali li jo e tenpo sike 2009 (Tu Ka Ka Lupo)pi pona mute.
mi wile e ni kin: wile ali pi sina mute li kama lon tenpo sike ni!
mi wile kin e ni: toki pona li kama jo e nimi sin pi mute lili.
          
[Happy 2009! (two oh oh nine, two thousand and nine)
May everyone have a very good 2009.
I also want all your wishes to come true this year.
I also want toki pona to get a few new words.]
