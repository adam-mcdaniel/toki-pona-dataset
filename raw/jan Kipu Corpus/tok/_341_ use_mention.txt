sitelen ma li ma ni ala. sama la nimi ijo li ijo ni ala. tan ni la nimi nimi li nimi ni ala. sina wile toki e ijo ijo la sina wile kepeken nimi pi ijo ni. tan ni la, sina wile toki e ijo nimi la sina wile kepeken nimi pi nimi ni li ken ala kepeken nimi sama. sina ken pali e nimi nimi kepeken nasin mute. taso nasin la nasin ni li pona pali: sina wile toki e ijo pi nimi ni: soweli; la sina kepeken nimi ni: `soweli'.

[A map is not that country.  Similarly, a word for a thing is not that thing.  So, the name of a word is not that word.  If you want to talk about a thing, you need to use a name of that thing.  Therefore, if you want to talk about a word, you need to use a name of that ord and cannot use that word itself.  You can make a name of a word in many ways. But, generally, this method is easy: If you want to talk about the word 
           cat
you use the word 
           `cat'.]
