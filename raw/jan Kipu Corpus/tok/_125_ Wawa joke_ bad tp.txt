[Just now a woman came to my store.  She wanted coffee.  I talked a little toki pona to her.  She said "Y' know, on the shore of the other ocean, there is a store called `Wawa'. The coffee at Wawa is very strong.  Ha ha ha!."
The time of knowing came.
Was she talking about this Wawa?
I don't know.  I am on the other coast.
Generally, `a a a a' means "don't do that".  `a a a' means feeling amused.  But is it true that you came to know another toki pona person?
Don't correct his expressions!
But people call this site "Toki pona only".  His words are not toki pona.
I think that we are friends.  Maybe I speak erroneously.  But you speak erroneously.  We want to give love to everybody.
Notice.  I didn't say that I will not correct your speech. 
Me too.  I want to give good too to everybody. If a person tries to write toki pona, one good for him is that I correct his erroneous speech.  I don't need this person to say "Thank you".  But I don't understand the reason that this person turns and fights me.
I am going to continue talking toki pona my way.
Fine.  But don't say that this talk is toki pona.
Kipo, you are not the creator of toki pona.  Even the creator doesn't have the language in her two hands.  Toki pona exists.  Toki pona is a language.  Languages become different.  People always exist and want to continue their language. But this man dies. His language continues in a different mouth and even changes a good deal.  
Listen to other languages.  Correct your language.  When other people come to hear your language, they correct their language.  This is the temporal path of language.
Indeed, when a long time passes, a language becomes different.  But when a really small time passes, a large part of a language remains.  Maybe in the past your usage was a part of toki pona.  (I think not very probably because we go rid of it a long time ago.) If this happened, it is not toki pona.  Maybe many people hear your talk and do the same.  After that, toki pona changes and uses your talking.  But not now.
No one can divide source toki pona and not toki pona like that.  Nobody has that power.  Sonja doesn't have that power.
Sonja is close.  But even she is not here.  I can tell you that the toki pona community does this.  People in that community even say bad things to people who do very different things.  You can want to not be in the group or you can want to change the usage of the group or you can do what you want without the group.  I can't make you do the one and not the other. But I can talk thoughtfully about your goals.  Do what you want.
