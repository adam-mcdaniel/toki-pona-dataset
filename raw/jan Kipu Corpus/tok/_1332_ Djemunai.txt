1332a
Christa Hansberry

ante toki pi lili lili...
"anpa li sewimonsi li sinpinala li sona e ni: ona li tawa semetaso ali li tawa mute."





[A very little translation.

Bottom is top.
Back is front.
Nothing know where it is going.
But everything moves a lot.]
