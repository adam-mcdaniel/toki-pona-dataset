sewi o selo e jan lili sina lon suno lon.
o pana sewi e mi lon ma jan.
o lawa e mi lon ma jan.
o suli e mi lon tenpo pi wile awen.
o pilin pona e mi lon pi pilin ike.
o sewi e mi lon tenpo anpa.
pona sina li suli.
sona mi li lili.
o pali e ni:
pona ni li awen lon insa mi.
pona ni li awen lon suno ali pi lon mi.
o pali e ni kepeken jan Kolisu.
o pali e ni kepeken jan lawa mi.
  Amen..

[O God, protect your children to day.
Bless me in the human world.
Guide me in the human world.
Enlarge me in the time of continuing need.
Cheer me in time of feeling bad.
Raise me in the time of being down.
Your goodness is great.
My knowledge is small.
Do this:
This goodness remains inside me.
Do this through Christ.
Do this through my Lord.
Amen.]


  'ali li sona ala e pona sina. o pona ni li awen poka mi lon tempo ali mi 
  kepeken jan lawa mi Jesu Kolisu.
  o ni li kama'

[Everyone does not know your goodness.  May this goodness remain with me all my days , through my Lord, Jesus Christ.]
