jan li esun e moku.  moku ni lipalisa soweli.  jan li tu e palisa li jo e sike soweli mute.
jan li jo e soweli tomo pi linja uta.  ona li soweli sike.
soweli sike li wile moku e sike soweli.  
taso soweli sike li pilin e ni: ike li ken kama.
ona la tawa weka.


[A man bought some food.  The food was a sausage. The man cut the cylinder and had many meat rounds.
The man had a cat. The cat was a round animal.  
The round animal wanted to eat the meat rounds.
But the round animal thought that bad things could happen.
He went away.]
