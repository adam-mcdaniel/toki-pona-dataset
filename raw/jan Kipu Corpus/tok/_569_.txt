  Depending on what you want to say. 'len jelo pi sitelen "10"' "yellow shirt with the label "10"" seems to cover the obvious case. They are the same, of course (at least usually), but they get there in different ways: 'jan pi musi sijelo, pi len pi sitelen "10"' and 'jan pi musi sijelo, "nanpa 10"' or something like that.
