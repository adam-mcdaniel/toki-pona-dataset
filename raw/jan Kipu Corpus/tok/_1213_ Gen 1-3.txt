Devan Andree

lipu Kenesi wan: sewi li pali e ma sewi e ma e soweli ali e jan. jan li sama lukin e sewi. jan li lawa e ma.

lipu Kenesi tu: sewi li pali e jan mije. sewi pana ma kasi Eten. jan li ken ala tawa kasi sona. jan Atan li wan. sewi li pali e jan meli.

lipu Kenesi tu wan: akesi linja li nasa e jan Ewe. jan Ewe en jan Atan li moku e kili pi kasi sona. ma ali kama ike. sewi pana weka jan Atan en jan Ewe.




1213a

kipisi nanpa wan pi lipu Kenesi: sewi li pali e ma sewi e ma e soweli ali e jan. jan li sama lukin sewi. jan li lawa e ma.
kipisi nanpa tu pi lipu Kenesi: sewi li pali e jan mije. sewi li pana e ma kasi Eten. jan li ken ala moku ta kasi sona. jan Atan li wan. sewi li pali e jan meli.
kipisi nanpa tu wan pi lipu Kenesi: akesi linja li nasa e jan Ewe. jan Ewe en jan Atan li moku e kili pi kasi sona. ma ali kama ike. sewi li pana weka e jan Atan e jan Ewe.








[Gen1: God created the heavens, the earth and everything that lives. He made humankind in his image, and gave them charge over the earth.
Gen2: God formed a man and gave him the garden in Eden, except the tree of good and evil. Adam was alone so God made a woman as his partner.
Gen3: The serpent deceived the woman. She and Adam ate from the tree. The ground was cursed, and God sent Adam and Eve out of the garden.]
