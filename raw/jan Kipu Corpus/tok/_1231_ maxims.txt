toki sona
                                                                               
toki sona li suli tawa nasin pi toki ali. tan ni la mi wile sitelen e toki sona pi pali kepeken toki pona. tenpo mute la toki ni li pona lon toki pali taso. jan li wile toki e sama kepeken toki ante la, toki li ike kalama. mi wile sitelen e ni tawa awen sona.jan sona li toki ala.jan toki li sona ala- jan Kipomi sona e ni taso:mi sona ala.- jan Alitotele (kepeken pi toki pona li tan mi)mi lukin e toki sona ante la, ken la mi ante e lipu ni.

Maxims
Maxims are important for the cultures of all languages.  So, I wanted to write a working maxim in 
toki pona. Often these saying are good only in the language they were made in. If a person wants to 
Say the same thing in another language, the saying sounds bad.  I want to write this for preserving 
the wisdom.

The wise man doesn't speak.
The talking man doesn't know
(jna Kipo)

I know only that I know nothing.
(Aristotle  in my toki pona)


If I see other maxims, maybe I will change this page.]
