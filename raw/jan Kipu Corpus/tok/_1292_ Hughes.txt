‎Joshua Birns-Sprague‎
mi toki ante e toki musi lili pi jan Lanton Ju.
akesi linja li ken moli e ijo --taso ona li moli ala e mi lon tenpo pini.
soweli wawa pi loje jelo en pimeja li ken moli e ijo --taso ona li moli ala moli e sina?
jan a li ken moli e ijo --ona li wile e ala la jan li awen moli e ijo.
jan li moli e soweli e sinatawa utala anu tawa musianu tawa moku a.






1292a

‎Joshua Birns-Sprague‎
mi  ante toki e toki musi lili pi jan Lanton Ju.
akesi linja li ken moli e ijo --taso ona li moli ala e mi lon tenpo pini.
soweli wawa pi loje jelo en pimeja li ken moli e ijo --taso ona li moli ala moli e sina?
jan a li ken moli e ijo --ona li wile e ala la jan li awen moli e ijo.
jan li moli e soweli e sinatawa utala anu tawa musianu tawa moku a.








[‎Joshua Birns-Sprague‎
I translated a poem by Langston Hughes.
A snake can kill something ...
But it didn't kill me.
A tiger can kill something ...
But did it kill you?
Even a man can kill something ...
If he wants nothing, a man can keep on killing things.
People kill animals and you 
For war or for fun 
Or for food even.]
