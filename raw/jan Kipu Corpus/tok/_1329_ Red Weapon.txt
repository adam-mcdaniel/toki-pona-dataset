RED WEAPON  --- ATTACK OF THE TITANS

Are you game?
No, I am a hunter.
Nobody knows
The name of a fallen flower.
A defeated bird wants
The arrival of good air.
If you only wish for an event,
Things don't change.
If you want change,
You need to act and fight.
This desire amuses the beasts.
I step over dead bodies and continue.
The goodness of animals, do this exist?
I want the freedom of willful snakes.
The evil of the metal box gives
My wiil and my desire to fight.
The quarry is dead
Against the great main walls because of the hunter.
The body burns and strongly wants
To kill its quarry!
It cuts the new sun with
The Red Weapon.]
