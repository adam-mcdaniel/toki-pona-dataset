[arono


Greetings! Now I have learned about this group.  Very good! Once upon a time, when toki pona was very new, I learned it.  But when a long time passed, I talked it a lot. Sonja (the creator of toki pona) is my friend. 
I am happy because of the existence of this group. When I speak toki  pona, I am happy.  My name is Ewen.  toki pona is a good language.]
