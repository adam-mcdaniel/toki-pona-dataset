Kalama musi pi tenpo pimeja, pi jan tawa
                                  lape li lon
                               sewi pi nena ali.
                             sina pilin lili e kon
                            lon lawa pi kasi suli.
                              waso li kalama ala
                             lon ma kasi. o awen.
                              tenpo kama lili la
                               sina kin li lape.
[Goethe, tp jan Jon.]

[Wanderer's night song

Peace is above every hill.
You can scarcely feel the breeze in the treetops.
The birds are silent in the woods.
Wait.  Soon you too will sleep. ]
