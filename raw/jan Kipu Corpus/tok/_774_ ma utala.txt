Sergey Tihonov
                                                                 12:38pm Nov 20
I wish you knew where I wrote this little poem. In the middle of the land occupied by Russia. In the so-called LPR. It was a season of heavy cold rains, no hope, no freedom, no money. Weaponed guys in camo everywhere, fear, death, darkness...I am in a safe place now, because it was my short business journey. But millions of people still live in this everlasting nightmare...

Color my hair with blue dye.
Go to the middle of a war country . Look at the people.

Only: love and fun and bread.
We come.  But we go.

"I exist and know and feel.  Color the hair
