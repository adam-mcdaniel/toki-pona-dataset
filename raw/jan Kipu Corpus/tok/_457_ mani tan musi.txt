   [jan Kala-
   What are you doing now?  I am eating.
   socks-
   I am getting money.
     jan Kala-
   How?
   socks-
   I play music.
   jan Kala-
   What?  I don't understand.
     jan Kala-
   Good.  But you said "I am getting money."
   Do you use money from music?
   socks-
   No.  I get money using music.  I am a musician.  If people give me money, I play music.
   jan Kala-
   Ahah!  Now I understand.
   jan Kala-
   My name uses sitelen pona
   
   This says "Isan".]
