LAWA PI "SERVER"

1. tenpo sin mute la o toki ala e ijo sama.
2. o utala ala e jan ante.
3. nasin pi jan lawa li nasin suli. o kute e ona.
4. tomo #toki li tomo pi toki pona taso. tomo #pana-e-sona en tomo #toki-lnli li tomo pi toki mute. (taso toki Inli en toki Epelanto en toki pona li pona mute lon tomo toki ni.)
5. o sitelen ala e toki utala ike. 

[1.  Don't say the same thing over and over again.
2. Don't fight with other people.
3. The administrator's ways are the important ways.  Listen to them.
4. The room #toki is the room for toki pona only. The room #pana-e-sona and the room #toki-Inli are rooms for many languages (but English and and Esperanto and toki pona are very good in these rooms).  
5.  Don't write bad hostile words.  ]
