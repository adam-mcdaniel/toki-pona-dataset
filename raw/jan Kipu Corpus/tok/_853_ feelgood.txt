Joshua Birns-Sprague

tenpo pi ale mi li open la mi pilin pona. mi pilin ike la mi weka e sona pi pilin pona mi. pilin pona li kon lon pi jan ale. jan o pilin pona!






[When the time of my life begins, I feel good.  If I feel bad, I discard knowledge of my good feelings.  Feeling good is the breath of life of all people.  People, feel good!]
