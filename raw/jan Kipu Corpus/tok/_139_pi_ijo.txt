    139a
    
    len ni li ijo mi ala. 
    tenpo ali la ma en ken en pona li ijo sina.
     tenpo ale la ma la ken la nimi li ijo sina. 
    kili ni li ijo mi. 
    toki Epelanto li ijo pi jan ali. 
    tomo li ijo mi lon insa pi ma tomo Kelejo, mi soon e ma tomo. 
    ilo ni li ijo sina. 
    ona li ijo pi jan lawa 
    pona.[Mato, 2014]
    
    [This cloth is not mine.
    Always, land and possibility and good are yours.
    Always, in the country, maybe the name is yours.
    This fruit is mine.
    Esperanto is everybody's.
    The house is mine inside the Calais. I know the city.
    This tool is yours.
    It is the ruler's.
    Good.]
    [The original is an old use of `pi'.  There some reasons for wanting it back.]
