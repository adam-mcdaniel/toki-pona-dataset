Marcus Miles

open suno la mi lukin e sewi suno lon telo suli lon insa pi ma sewi. mi jo e tomo lon ma sewi. tomo li jo e supa kiwen lon selo pi tomo mi. mi musi kepeken musi supa lon ma ni. mi moku e moku lili e telo nasa lon ma ni.

Marcus Miles mi ante e toki li suli e toki. tempo ale la mi pana e pilin pona tan ni: jan pi toki pona li pana e sona pona.


[In the morning I look at the rising sun in the lake in the middle of the high country.  I have a house in the high country.  The house has a stone table outside my home.  I play board games there.  I eat a little food and drink booze there.  
I translated and expanded this message.  I always give a good feeling because toki pona people give good knowledge.  ]
