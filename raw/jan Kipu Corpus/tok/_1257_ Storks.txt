1257a

Piotrek Zadwornytelo Sesa li telo e ma,e ma, e kasi lili.ma ni li tomo mi, li tomo pi waso walo sewi.tawa ma pi waso walo sewi.mi mute li lon tomo ala,li sama waso walo sewi.tawa  ma pi waso walo sewi.







[Piotrek ZadwornyThe river Sesa waters the land.,
The land and the little plants.
This country is my home and the home of great white birds (storks)

To the land of the storks!

We are not at home
And are like the storks.

To the land of the storks!]
