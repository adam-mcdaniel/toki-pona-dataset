[Egg

You died while driving.
The cause of death was an auto accident.  It wasn't big.  But you were dead.  You had a wife and two children.  You didn't feel the injuries.  The medics worked hard.  But they did not dispel death.  This death was good for you because your body was very very severely mangled. 

Now you come to me.

You ask "What... what happened.  Where am I?"
I say matter-of-factly "You're dead".  I want to speak the truth.
"I saw a ... a truck.  It was moving weirdly..."
I say "Yes"
"Me ... am I dead?"
I say "Yes. But don't feel bad because of that.  Everybody dies",

You look around.  There is no ground here.  Only you and me exist.  You ask "What is this place? Is it the land of dead people?"  
I say "It is like that."
You ask "Are you God?"
I say "Yes.  I am God."

You say "My children ... My wife ..."
"What about them matters?"
"Are the happy?"
I say "I like this.  You have just died.  But you think about your family.  You are a good person."

You look closely at me.  I don't look like a god to you.  I look like some guy.  Maybe I look like a policeman.  Maybe I look like a teacher.  I don't look like an omnipotent person. 
I say "Don't feel bad, they are happy.  Your children think that you were very good.  They did not have time to despise you.  Your wife cries.  Only inside is she happy.  I think the marriage of you and her was falling apart.  Maybe you should feel good because she feels bad about feeling good about your departure."

You say "Oh.  Now what happens?  Do I go to Heaven or to Hell or what?"  
I say "No.  I will renew your life."
You say "Oh! Hindus got it right."
I say "Every religion gets it right.  Walk with me."
You walk with me.  We walk in the void.  "Where are we going?"
I say "We are going nowhere i particular.  But when we talk, I like to walk."
You ask "Why do you do this?  When you renew my life, do I become an unknowing person.  I will be an infant.  All my life and all my deeds are not important."
I say "They are important.  You have knowledge of all your lives inside yourself.  But now you don't know them."
I stop my walk and touch your shoulder.  "You cannot know all this: that your soul is very good and very great.  Human understanding can hold only a very tiny part.  It's like this: You put your finger in a glass because you want to know whether the water is cold or hot.  You put a very tiny part of yourself into the glass.  When you withdraw it from the glass, it give you complete knowledge of the glass.  For a long time, you are in a human body.  You don't know that your soul is very great.  If we stayed here, you would recall everything.  But it is not good for us to stay forever."  

How often have you renewed my life?
I say "Oh, many times.  Very many times.  I have given bodies of many other people. Now, in your next life, you are a Chinese girl and work with plants and animals a long long time ago." 
You say thoughtfully "Oh, what?  Are you sending me to the past?"
"Oh, that is like the truth.  You understand time. But time is in your universe only. In my word, things are different."
"Your world?"
 I say clearly "Oh, yes.  I come from a world.  I come from another world.  There are other gods in that world.  I know that you want to know what that world looks like.  But, if I tell you, you can't understand it."
You say a little peevishly "But listen to me! If I go to a different time, it is possible that sometime I would talk to myself."
"Yes. This happens often.  If you two each know only one of your lives, you don't know that you are talking to yourself."  
   
"What is the reason for everything?"
I say "Oh, you ask this?  You want to know what is the reason for everything?  Everyboy wants to know this."
You say, keeping good, "I want to know the reason."
I look into your eyes.  "The reason for everything and the reason for this universe is for you to learn."
"Are you talking about everybody? Do you want us to learn?"
"No.  I want just you to learn.  I made this universe for you alone.  When you exist again you become greater and learn."
"Just me?  Did you make universes for other people?"
I say "There are no other people.  Only you and I are in this universe."
You look weakly at me "But everybody in the world ..."
"Everybody is you.  Everybody is a different you."
"Oh, go one.  I am everybody?"
I say "Yes.  Now you know."  I touch your shoulder because I feel good.
"Was I everybody in the past?"
"Everybody.  And in the future, to, you are everybody."
"I was Abraham Lincoln.?"
I say "You were John Wilkes Booth too."
You say fearfully "I was Hitler?"
"Hitler killed very very very many people.  You were all those people too."
"I was Jesus?"
"Many people went with him.  You were those people too."

You stop your talking.
I say "If you hurt somebody, you hurt yourself.  If you help somebody, you help yourself.  If anybody feels happy or sad, you feel the same."

For a long time you think about this.
You ask "Why did you do this?"
"So that sometime you become the same as me.  You will be the same as me.  You are my child."
You say thoughtfully "Oh, am I a god?"
"You are not a god yet.  You are an unstarted god.  You really enlarge yourself.  You always contain the life of everybody.  When this happens, you are great and begin your life."
You say "The whole universe is ..."
I say too "Just an egg.  Now I want to renew your life."
I send you on your path.
