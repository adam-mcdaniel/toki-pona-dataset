"I stand", "I sit" "I lie down"

[How does one say in toki pona "I stand", "I sit", "I lie"?
Maybe many things work.  But many people use `mi kama sinpin', `mi anpa e monsi (mi)', `mi supa'.
I like `mi (kama) sinpin' and `mi (kama) supa'.
I think that `mi (kama) supa e noka' is very good for "sit"]
