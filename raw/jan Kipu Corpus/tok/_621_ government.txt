jan pi toki pona li kama e kulupa lawa ma la, mi mute li pali e kulupu anu nasin pi sama seme?
  
  John Clifford 
  ken la 'kama kulupu'.

John Clifford 
ni li nasa ni: mi kena ala pilin e ijo ona. 

Marie Elliott 
ken la nasin lawa kulupu li pona. nasin lawa mani li ike. ni li tan: mi mute li jo ala e nimi mute tawa mani en nanpa anu seme XDjan lawa anu kulupu lawa li wile sama ni: ona li pona mute tawa jan mute. jan li wile e nasin la jan lawa anu kulupu lawa li pali e nasin ni. jan lawa anu kulupu lawa li wile ala wawa ike mute li wile pona tawa jan, li wile sona e lon jan. jan lawa li sona e ijo pi lon pi jan pi mani lili (pi pi pi a ~o.o~) la jan ni li sona e nasin ni: ona li pona e ijo lon e ijo mani. anu la, ken la jan lawa li pilin ala mute e ijo pi lon pi jan ni li wile ala mute, pona e ona.mi pilin e ijo ni: ni li pona tawa kulupu lawa en jan, pi ma ni: jan li pona e ma kasi li pali e ma kili lon tomo ona. mi mute li ken moku pona, li wile ala kama jo e moku mute tan ma ante kepeken mani mute. ni kin: kon jaki pi tan tomo tawa en tomo ilo pali li ike. mi mute o tawa noka anu tawa soweli, o pali e ijo pi pona pi tawa ma, kepeken luka en ilo pona.sina seme pilin a? 

Kipo: 
'nimi mute pi (tawa) mani...'  kulupu pini ni li wile sona ala.  tan ni la nimi 'anu seme' li lon ala. sina wile toki e seme?  'wawa pi ike mute'? '(pi pi pi a ~o.o ~)'  sitelen pilin?  nimi 'anu la' li lon ala (mi pilin).

If tokiponists created a government, would we make a group or what sort of system?
John Clifford 
This is so crazy, I can't think about it.
Marie Elliott 
Maybe the group government is good.  The money government is bad.  This is because we don't have many words for money and numbers.  The leader or the ruling group want something like the most good for the most people.  If people want a system, the leader or the governing group make that system.  The leader or governing group does not want much bad weakness and good things for people and want to improve people's lives. If the leader knows about the lives of poor people, he knows this way: he improves the things of life and the things of money.  Or, maybe the leader does not think much about the lives of these people and does not much want to fix them.
I think about this: This is good for the government and people of this country: people improve fields and make orcards at their homes.  We can eat well and do not need to get a lot of food from other countries for a lot of money.  This, too: make things good for the land using hands and good tools.  What do you think?
