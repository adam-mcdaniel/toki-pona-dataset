'lipu esun pi tomo tawa suli' (toki musi):
tomo telo li lon telo tawa.
tawa tenpo pi kama jan la ona li awen. 
tenpo ni la ona li wile tawa weka.
tenpo awen pi tomo tawa suli la
jan wan li lukin e tomo telo ni - 
e tomo telo pakala mute,
lon poka pi nasin tawa.
jan ni li pilin e ni:
ona li wile kama lon insa
li wile tawa e tomo telo,
lon nasin pi telo kiwen,
lon poka pi tomo tawa pi tawa sike,
li wile weka e lipu esun ona pi tomo tawa suli, 
tawa kon.
  
  Antônio Lucas Araújo Moreira tomo telo li wile tawa tan ni anu seme: ona li awen la ona li kama pakala sama tomo telo ante?
  telo kiwen li seme? ona li lete anu jo e kiwen mute?
  tomo tawa li tawa sike la ona li pakala ala pakala?
  
  jan ni li kama ala kama jo e tomo tawa suli lon esun? ona li wile weka e lipu esun tan seme?
  Like

  Ryan Moniz 1) sina li toki e tan lon pi wile ona.

  3) tomo telo li lon ala lon....
  4) mi sona ala e kama lon pi jan ni.

Brochure for a big car (poem)

The toilet is in the river.
For the time of of the coming of a man it waits.
Now it wants to go away.
For the running time of the big car,
One man sees this toilet  --
And many ruined toilets
At the side of the highway.
This man thinks that
He wants to get inside
And wants to move the toilet
In the path of the solid river,
Beside the car which goes in circles, 
And wants to release his brochure for the bigg car
On the wind. 
tawa kon.
  
  Antônio Lucas Araújo Moreira
  Does this toilet want to go from here?  If it remains, it will become ruined like the other toilets?

  If the car goes in circles, is it ruined?
