[What is food/eats?
Everything
If you know this, you know your life.
I am food/eat
You are food/eat
Plants are food/eat.
Stones are food.
All air/breeathing things are warm.
If something is not warm, it is dead.
Everything is food and warm.
I am warm.
Air/breath is warm.
I consume air.
In the future, air will consume me.
When I consume air, air waits for me.
When air consume me, I wait for air.
In these two ways, air and I are food and warm and one.]
