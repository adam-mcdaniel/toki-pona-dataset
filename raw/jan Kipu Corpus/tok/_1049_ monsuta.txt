nimi "monsuta" li ike tawa jan mute. taso ona li pona tawa mi tan ni: nimi "monsuta" li ken e ni: sina toki e ijo pi pilin "fear". nimi monsuta li nimi "threat" kepeken toki Inli. sina wile toki e nimi "I am afraid", la sina ken toki e nimi "mi pilin e monsuta". ni li nimi "I think about a threat" kepeken toki Inli. nasin sin li lon ala. tan ni la nimi "monsuta" li pona tawa mi.







[Stephen Waldron
"Monsuta"
Many people dislike the word `monsuta', but I like it because it enables you to talk about the feeling of fear.  
`monsuta' is "threat" in English.  If you want to say "I am afraid", you can say  `mi pilin e monsuta' which is "I feel a threat" in English.  There isn't a new method.  So I like `monsuta'.]
