1336a
Chris Howlett

jan lili mute en jan wan pi lili mute li tawa kiwen suli tan ni: jan lili mute ni li kama jo e mani mute tan akesi suli. jan pi wawa nasa li pana e pona tawa jan lili mute ni.
jan pi lili mute ni li kama jo e sike lili pi kiwen mani pi wawa nasa ni: sike ni li lon luka jan la jan ala li ken lukin e jan ni.







[Chris Howlett

Several little people and one very little person went to a big stone because the very little person got a lot of money from a big dragon. A person with magical powers helped the very little person.
The very little person got a little gold ring with this magical power: when this ring was on a person's hand, no one could see him.]
