651a

Chris Howlett‎ to toki pona

tenpo mute la mi pali musi li toki musi li kalama musi lon tomo musi tawa jan mute. jan mute ni li lukin e mi e jan ante pi pali musi. mi mute li pona tawa jan lukin. jan lukin li a a a.
jan wan li lawa e jan mute pi pali musi. ona li lawa e ni tawa mi mute: mi mute li tawa ona.
tenpo pimeja ni la mi toki musi li kalama musi tawa jan pi lawa musi, tan ni: mi wile pali musi pi nasin ni: mi jan sewi pi sitelen musi.
o toki e ni tawa mi: 'o pali pona!'



[Often, i make music and sing and play in music halls for many people.  These people see me and other musicians .  The observers like us.The observers laugh.
One person leads the band.  He decides where we go for us.
Tonight I am singing and playing for the band leaders because I want to play the sort of music that I am master of.
Say "Do well!" for me.]
