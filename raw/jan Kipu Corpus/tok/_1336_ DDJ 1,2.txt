Joop  Kiefte
一章
道，可道也，非恆道也。名，可名也，非恆名也。 "無"，名天地之始；"有"，名萬物之母。 故，常"無"，欲以觀其妙；常"有"，欲以觀其徼。 此兩者，同出而異名，同謂之玄。玄之又玄，眾妙之門。

toki wan

nasin li ken nasin, li nasin awen ala. nimi li ken nimi, li nimi awen ala. nimi Weka li kama pi kon en ma. nimi Jo li mama pi ijo ale. ni la, o wile weka, o kepeken, o lukin e sona. o wile jo, o kepeken, o lukin e awen. ona tu li sama li nimi ante, li pimeja sama, li pimeja pi pimeja kin, li lupa mute sona.
二章
天下皆知美之為美，斯惡已。皆知善之為善，斯不善已。有無相生，難易相成，長短相形，高下相盈，音聲相和，前後相隨。恒也。是以聖人處無為之事，行不言之教；萬物作而弗始，生而弗有，為而弗恃，功成而不居。夫唯弗居，是以不去。

[First piece

The way that can be a path is not the continuing way.  The word that can be a name is not the continuing word. The word Away is the beginning of of air and earth.  The word Have is the source of everything.  In this case, desire to be away, be useful, look at wisdom, want to have, be useful, look at the continuing.  These two are the same but different words, the same darkness, even the dark of darkness, and the many doors to wisdom.

Second piece

If everything under the air knows beauty because of the beautiful, that is not beautiful.  If everything knows virtue because of the virtuous, that is not virtue.  Have and Away create these two.  Bad and Good bring these two.  Many and Few send these two. High and Low confines these two.  Discourse and Noise classify these two. Front and Back move these two.  Always.  So the holy man is away from action.  His life is not holy talk.  Everything is great and become nothing, and exists and doesn't possess, and is an origin and not a goal and ends well and does not continue.  Thoughts don't continue.  So, motion!]
