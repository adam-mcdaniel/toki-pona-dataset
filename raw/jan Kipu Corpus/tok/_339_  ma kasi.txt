[Greetings, everybody.  There are little creatures and trees and running water in the meadow.  If you were here, what would you want to do?
If I were warm, I would want to be in the running water.  If I were cool, I woul want to look at the animals and birds.
In daylight, I would go into the trees.  At light, I would build a fire.  Always, I would drink in the good air.
Oh, all these things are very good.  I am looking for the beginning of the running water.  This is a good and a high for me.}
