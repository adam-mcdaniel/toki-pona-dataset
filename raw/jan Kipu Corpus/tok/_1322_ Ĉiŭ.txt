Bluish white bird.

I am a bluish white bird.
I sing,
To you only.
Lady, listen 
To me.  Listen!
Know that
You are very sweet.
If she (you) are an animal,
Then I am a snake.
If I want a fruit,
She (you) are a tree.
If she (you) is the ocean
I am am a fish.
If I don't have her (you),
I have nothing.

John Clifford
Very nice.  But the metaphors are a little hard to understand. ]
