[‎Maxime Wegesin‎

A great fire burns inside me
A great fire burns in my soul.
In the future, this fire
Will enlarge me
And will improve me
And will be a light for me.
seli suli li seli lon insa mi.seli suli li seli lon kon sewi mi.
In the future, this fire
Will destroy me
And will degrade me
And will darken my soul.
Oh, everybody has their inner fire.
Everybody is divine.  Everybody feels bad.
A divine joke!]
