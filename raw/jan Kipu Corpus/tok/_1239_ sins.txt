Jan-David
Pakala Lili: pakala lili li lili ala li mute mute tawa jan sewi suli Jawe. pakala ali li ike tan Jawe li olin ala e ona mute. mi mute li wile pali e wile pi Jawe. Kiwen lili li ken pali e kiwen weka utala mute. Tan mi mute li wile utala e pakala lili e pakala mute.






1239a
Jan-David
Pakala Lili: pakala lili li lili ala li suli mute tawa sewi Jawe. pakala ali li ike tawa sewi Jawe. mi mute li wile pali e wile pi sewi Jawe. kiwen lili pi tawa anpa li ken kama e ni: kiwen suli li kama anpa li pakala  mute.  tan ni la mi mute li wile utala e pakala lili e pakala mute.







[Little Sins.
Little sins are not little but are very big to the Lord.  The Lord dislikes all sins.  We want to do the Lord's will.  Little stones rolling down can cause large stones to come down and do great damage.  So, we need to fight little sins and big ones.]
