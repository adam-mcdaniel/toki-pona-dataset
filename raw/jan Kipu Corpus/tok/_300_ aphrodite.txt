sewi meli Aposite pi len ala

jan alasa Akiton li tawa alasa e soweli palisa poka soweli alasa ona.  ona li kama lukin e ni: sewi meli Aposite li telo e sama.  ona li jo e len ala. jan Akiton li awen kin lukin. tan ni la sewi meli Aposite li kama pilin ike li soweli e ona.  jan Akiiton li kama soweli palisa.  soweli alasa ona li alasa li moku li moli, e ona.  

jan pi sitelen kiwen Pasitele li sitelen e kiwen sitelen pi sewi meli Aposite.  ona li pona ni: sewi meli Aposite li toki e nimi "a! Jna Pasitele o, tenpo seme la sina lukin e mi pi len ala?"

[Aphrodite without her nightie.

Acteon the hunter went hunting a stag with his hunting dogs.  He happened to see Aphrodite taking a bath.  She was naked.  Acteon just kept looking. So Aphrodite got mad and turned him into a beast.  He became a stag.  His hunting dogs chased, ate and killed him.

The sculpture Praxiteles carved a statue of Aphrodite.  It was so good that Aphrodite  said "My, Praxiteles, when did you see me naked?"]
