tenpo suno ni li tenpo suno pi toki pi mama meli. o toki sama ni: o pali e pali pi tomo sona! o pona e ma lape sina! tenpo ala la sina pana e toki tawa mi kepeken ilo toki. o pana e toki tawa mi kepeken ilo toki! o tawa o pana e wile sona ni tawa mama mije sina.



[Today is the day of mother language.  Talk like this:
Do your school work.
Clean your room.
You never talk to me on the phone.
Talk to me on the phone!
Go and ask this question of your mother.]
