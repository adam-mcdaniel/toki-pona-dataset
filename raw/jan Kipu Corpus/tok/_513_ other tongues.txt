tenpo pini la mi kama sona e toki pona. ni li pona mute tawa mi.

[A long time ago, I cared little for other languages.
A long time ago, I went to Argentina and learned Spanish.  I liked that a lot.
A while ago, I learned toki pona.  I like that a lot.
Now I want to learn other languages.  Many of them.
I feel good because of learning the ways of speaking of other languages.
I think that I like all other languages.  Good!]
