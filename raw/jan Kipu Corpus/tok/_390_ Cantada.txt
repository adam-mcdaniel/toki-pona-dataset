[Love Talk
If I have you,
What would I want to know the time for?
Is the time dark or hot?
Isn't this the warm time?
Did the sun come or not?
What is music like this for?
If I have you, what is a musician for?
God and questions
And road trees are for what?
What is a path for?
I have you.]

My rough translation:
After having you,
Why* wanting to know what time is it?
If it's night or it's hot,
If we're in the Summer,
If the Sun will come or not,
Or what is a song like this for?
After having you, what are poets for?
The gods, the doubts, 
What are almond trees** on the streets for?
What are the streets for?
After having you...

* Actually, "what for"
** I didn't want to specify trees or almonds because I didn't want to have too many modifiers on "kasi". Same for just "nasin" instead of "pi lon nasin".]
