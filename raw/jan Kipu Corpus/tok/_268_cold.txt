268a

mun pimeja li kama lete.
tomo li lete. 
ona mije li lape lon supa.
mi lape poka ona.
supa li lete. taso ona li seli.

[The dark moon turns cold
The house is cold.
He sleeps on the bed.
I sleep with him.
The bed is cold.  But he is warm.]
