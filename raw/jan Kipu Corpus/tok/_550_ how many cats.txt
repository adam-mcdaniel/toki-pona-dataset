[A scholar and a student are talking
Scholar:
You have two friends.  They have many cats.  The first friend gives you one cat.  The other friend gives you two cats.  How many cats do you get?
Student
Four.
Sch.
No.  Listen carefully.  You get one cat and two cats from your friends.  What is the number of cats?
St.
Just four.
Sch
Fine.  Change things.  If you get oe fruit and two fruits, you get how many fruits? 
St.
Three
Sch.
Because of what mixup do you say this? The number of cats was four. 
St:
From this mixup: I now already have a cat at home. ]
