kama ante sewi

jan Pita li sona e ni: jan Jesu li jan Kolisu. tenpo suno luka wan li pini. jan Jesu li sewi ma sewi. jan poka ona tu wan li tawa. kon walo li pali e ni: jan ala li ken lukin e ijo ala. jan sewi li tawa anpa tan sewi. jan ni li jan Elija en jan Mose. jan Pita li pilin e ni: mi pi tu wan li pali e tomo tawa sewi tawa jan sewi pi tu wan. kalama pi lawa sewi Atone li tawa anpa li toki. ona li toki e ni: jan Jesu li jan lili mi. mi pana e ona. jan Pita li anpa tawa ma kepeken uta. kon walo li ala. jan Elija en jan Mose en kalama pi lawa sewi Atone li weka. jan Jesu li toki tawa jan poka ona li toki e ni: o toki e lukin sina tawa jan ala.





1325a

Marcus Miles

kama ante sewi

jan Pita li sona e ni: jan Jesu li jan Kolisu. tenpo suno luka wan li pini. jan Jesu li tawa sewi nena. jan  tu wan li tawa poka ona. kon walo li kama e ni: jan li ken lukin e ijo ala. jan sewi pi Elija en Mose li tawa anpa tan sewi.. jan Pita li toki e ni: mi  tu wan o pali e tomo sewi tawa tu wan tawa jan sewi tu wan. kalama pi lawa sewi li tawa anpa li toki e ni: jan Jesu li jan lili mi; mi pana e ona. jan Pita li anpa tawa ma. kon walo li tawa weka. jan Elija en jan Mose en kalama pi lawa sewi li tawa weka. jan Jesu li toki tawa jan pi poka ona li toki e ni: o toki e lukin sina tawa jan ala. 








[Transfiguration.

Peter knew that Jesus was the Christ. Six days had passed. Jesus went up a mountain.  Three men went with him.  A cloud caused the men to see nothing.  The prophets Elijah and Moses went down from Heaven.  Peter said "Let us three make three tabernacles for the three holy men. The voice of the Lord came down and said "Jesus is my child.  I sent him."  Peter bowed to the ground.  The cloud went away.  Elijah and Moses and the voice of the Lord went away.  Jesus spoke to the people with him and said "Tell your vision to no one."]
