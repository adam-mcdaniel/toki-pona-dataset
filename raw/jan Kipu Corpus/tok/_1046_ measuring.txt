[I am talking about this way of measuring, called "metric" in English.  Every country except America uses one system of measurements.  The laws of America also use this system.  But generally the people of America use several different systems. They use one system to measure length and a different system to measure volume and yet another system to measure liquids and a new system to measure temperature.  This is crazy.  Enforce the law. ]
