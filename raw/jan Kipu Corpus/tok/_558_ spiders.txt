‎Joshua Birns-Sprague‎


pipi pi noka luka tu wan li mama pi pipi lili kama mute.pipi lili li lon insa pi len pipi.len pipi li lon anpa pi ilo musi.jan lili tu mi li tawa sewi pi ilo musi kepeken nasin monsi.tan sewi pi ilo musi la ona tu li tawa noka kepeken nasin sinpin.nasin sinpin li musi li sama lipu kiwen suli.ona tu li wile tawa anpa pi ilo musi lon poka pipi.taso mi lon insa pi ilo musi en jan lili.mi wile ala e ni: ona tu li lon poka pipi.pipi li pona tawa mi. tan ni la mi wile awen e pipi e pipi kama.
[A spider parents many future little spiders.
The little spiders are in a spider web.
The spider web is under a fun instrument.
My two children go to top of the instrument on the back path.
From the top they walk on the front path.
The front path is fun and like a big stone pad.
They want to go under the fun instrument next to the spiders.
But I am between the fun instrument and the children.
I don't want the two of thm to be next to the spiders.
I like spiders.  So I want to preserve spiders and future spiders.]
