Remco Beckers

mi sona e ni: waso awen li mu tan semetan jan Maja Anjelo
waso tawa wawa li lon sewi pi monsipi kon tawa. ona li kama tawa noka.tawa kon nasin pini, la ona li opene luka pi tawa sewi ona lon sunojelo loje. ona li ken lawa e sewi.







[Remco Beckers
I know this Why does the kept bird sing?
By Maya Angelou

The strongly moving bird is above the back 
of the wind.  It comes toward the base,
Toward the final breath of air, it opens
Its wings in the orange sun.
It can rule the sky.


I know why the caged bird sings

A free bird leaps
   on the back of the wind   
   and floats downstream   
   till the current ends
   and dips his wing
   in the orange sun rays
   and dares to claim the sky.]
