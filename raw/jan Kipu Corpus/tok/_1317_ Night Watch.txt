1317a

Joshua Birns-Sprague

tenpo pimeja li kama la tenpo lukin mi li open.mi moli ala la tenpo lukin mi li pini ala.mi en meli li kama ala wan.mi lawa ala e ma.mi mama ala pi jan lili.mi len ala e sama kepeken len pi jan lawa.mi sewi ala e samai.mi lon pali mi. mi moli lon pali mi.mi palisa utala lon pimeja.mi jan lukin lon sinpin suli.mi selo pi ma jan.mi pana e samai tawa kulupu ni: jan li lukin lon tenpo pimeja ale.










[When night comes, my watch begins.
If I am not dead, my watch is not done.
I and a woman do not marry.
I rule no land.
I parent no children.
I do not cloth myself in royal robes.
I do not exalt myself. 
I live in my job.  I die in my job.
I am the sword in the darkness.
I am the watcher on The Wall.
I am the protection of human lands.
I give myself to this group: the people who watch every night. 






Night gathers, and now my watch begins. It shall not end until my death. I shall take no wife, hold no lands, father no children. I shall wear no crowns and win no glory. I shall live and die at my post. I am the sword in the darkness. I am the watcher on the walls. I am the shield that guards the realms of men. I pledge my life and honor to the Night's Watch, for this night and all the nights to come.]
