1234a

a mi lukin e mama mije mi lon nia mi lukin e mama mile mi e meli sama mi e mije sama mi lon ni.a mi lukin e linja pi tawa open pi jan mi a ona mute li toki miona ali li toki e ni tawa mi: o kama tawa lon sina pi lon tomo Wawala. tenpo ali la jan wawa li awen lon tomo ni.






[The Viking Prayer from "The 13th Warrior" moved me enough that I memorized it:Lo there do I see my Father.Lo there do I see my Mother and my Sisters and my Brothers.Lo there do I see the Line of My People, back to the beginning. Lo they do call to me.They bid me take my place among them, in the Halls of Valhala where the Brave may live forever.]
