*786*

Dave Raftery

tenpo suno li kama lili.  lete lili li lon.  kasi mi li kama moli. ma kasi mi li pini tawa tenpo sike ni,  mi pilin ike. mi weka e kas moli. mi weka e kasi ike. mi tawa e ona tawa nene pi ma pona.  Mi pana e ma pona tawa ma kasi mi.  tenpo pini lili la ma kasi mi li pali e kasi [Tomatoes] e kasi [Carrots] e kasi [Beans]. tenpo sike pini la mi pana e kasi loje lili pi tu tu.  tenpo ni la suli pi kasi loje li sama mi.

Pusa:
mi lon pimeja.
wan taso la mi awen
tan olin sina.





786a

tenpo suno li kama lili.  lete lili li lon.  kasi mi li kama moli. ma kasi mi li pini tawa tenpo sike ni,  mi ike pillin. mi weka e kasi moli. mi weka e kasi ike. mi tawa e ona tawa nena pi ma pona.  Mi pana e ma pona tawa ma kasi mi.  tenpo pini lili la ma kasi mi li pali e kasi [Tomatoes] e kasi [Carrots] e kasi [Beans]. tenpo sike pini la mi pana e kasi loje lili tu tu.  tenpo ni la suli pi kasi loje li sama mi.

Pusa:
mi lon pimeja.
wan taso la mi awen
tan olin sina.






[Days are getting shorter.  There is a chill.  My plants are dying.  My garden is finished for the year.  I feel sad. I discard dead plants.  I discard weeds.  I move them to the compost pile.  I spread compost on my garden.  A little while ago, my garden made tomatoes and carrots and beans.  Last year, I planted four little redwood tree.  Now they are as tall as I am.  

I am in darkness
One alone, only I remain
From your love. ]
