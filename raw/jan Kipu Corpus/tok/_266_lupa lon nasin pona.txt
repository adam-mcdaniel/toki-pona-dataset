Dominick DiMercurio II
mi la nasin pona li jo e lupa.

Zoltán Gorza mi wile e ni: sina wile e meli

Dominick DiMercurio II lupa ni ala. lupa pi sona. mi toki seme e ni?
  
  Piotr Mittelstaedt lupa pi sona pi ijo pini? 
  anu lupa sona ni: ona li pana e nasin insa ken tawa jan ?
  e

Dominick DiMercurio II mi sona ala.
  
  Dominick DiMercurio II mi wile toki e ni: ike li pona, pona li ike.
  
  Piotr Mittelstaedt mi pilin e ni:
  tenpo mute la ijo pona li jo e lupa lili ike. jan li pilin e ni: jan li sona li pona la jan li suli. lupa li kama suli la ni ali li kama ike.
  
  ijo ike li jo lupa lili pona. taso jan li pilin e ni: ni ali li ike. tan ni la pona lili li ike tawa jan ni.

[In my opinion, the good path has a hole.
 I think that you need a woman.
Not that hole. A hole in knowledge.  How do I say this?
A hole in the knowledge of final things?  Or this knowledge hole: it gives a possible interior path for people?
I don't know.
I want to say that bad is good.  Good is bad.
I think that often a good thing has a little bad hole.  People think that if a person is wise and good, he is great.  If the hole becomes big, all this becomes bad.  
A bad thing has a little good hole.  But people think that all this is bad.  So a little good is bad for these people.]
