[Joshua Birns-Sprague
Yesterday, i bought a musical tube.  In it two little slips of wood  sound somehting and are at the end of the tube.  You have to mouth the two little slips.  You have to blow the tube.  The tube has many holes.  Using your two hands you can change the sound of the tube. This is great fun. 
 
James Crom 
An oboe?

Joshua Birns-Sprague 
James, that is the right word.  But use only toki pona on this site.]
