Leo Vrizcraya 
 mi kama sona e toki pona tan ni: mi wile sona sin. tenpo ni la mi sona e ni: toki pona li pana e kalama tawa pilin mi. mii sitelen e pilin mi lon musi kepeken toki pona la ni li pona tawa mi tan ni: pilin pi ike mi en pimeja mi li lili. tenpo sike tu tu tu tu wan (9) la toki pona li toki pi pilin mi.
:
 Rick Steenblik 
 pona mute!




[Leo Vrizcraya 
I learned toki pona because I wanted to know more.  Now i know that toki pona gives a voice to my feelings.  When I write my feelings for fun using toki pona, I like it because my bad feelings and my dark ones are small.  For nine years toki pona has been the language of my feelings.]

 Rick Steenblik 
Thanks you a lot.!]
