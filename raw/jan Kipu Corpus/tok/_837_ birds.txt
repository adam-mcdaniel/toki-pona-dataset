I hear that the Canadian geese are calling in the river.  I know that summer is coming.

Sarah Brown 
Oh, at my daughter's I see these birds.  They drop lots of goose goo on the banks.

Anne Campbell 
Today, the birds stayed in the river.  I could not see goose goo.


Sarah Brown 
Good!  Nobody wants to see that.  But the birds are strong and good looking. 

Anne Campbell 
Indeed!  I like them.  When these birds come, summer is coming. 

Sarah Brown
