DARKNESS OF TIME
(THOUGHTS ON THE BANKS OF THE NEVA)
The water flow like the darkness of time.
The water is the Neva.  The darkness of time continues.
Continuing is good for Petersburg.
If Petersburg wants, time will not go.
What do the people of Petersburg want?
Eyes see the course of the rive.
The line goes to the end from the future.
A house sees a house. I am like something different.
