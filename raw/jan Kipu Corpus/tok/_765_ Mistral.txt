jan Kapijela Mita 


[The stars aer a circle of children. They look at the earth in the game,
Grain fields are cloths of girls.  They circle in the game.
Rivers are circles of children.  They seek other children in the game.
Roaring seas are a circle of girls.  They embrace the word in the game.]
