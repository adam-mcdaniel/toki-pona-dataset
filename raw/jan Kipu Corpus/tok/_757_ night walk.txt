[Niko Bell

A little poem

The town is asleep
Water comes down from the sky
Like a big black blanket.
I am all alone
In the path of a yellow flashlight beam.
I inhale cold air,
I exhale white clouds.
But I know that 
New warm weather is coming
In a little while.  
  
  Marco Puddu 
  I like your poem a lot.  But I don't understand `suno ilo jelo'..


Christa Hansberry 
If a flashlight casts a yellow light, it is `suno ilo jelo'
Marco Puddu 
Ah1  Now I understand! Thanks Christa!

Serge Ganachaud 
I he you sleep well.  Like you, I wait for the warm time.  I feel good because it will come in a little while.
