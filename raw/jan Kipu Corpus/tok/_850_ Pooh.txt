Christa Hansberry ni li toki musi pi soweli Pu:

mi lukin e soweli Pike la,
tenpo suno mi li ken pona.
mi lukin ala e soweli Pike la,
tenpo suno mi li ken ala pona.
ni li suli ala tawa mi:
mi lukin ala e waso Owa anu soweli Ejo
(anu soweli ante),
kin la mi lukin ala e waso Owa anu soweli Ejo
(anu soweli ante)
anu jan Kitipo Lopin.

This is a Pooh poem
If I see Piglet,
My day can be good.
If I don't see Piglet,
My day cannot be good.
These are not important to me:
I see Owl or Eeyore
(or another animal),
