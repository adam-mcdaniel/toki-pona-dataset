Ryan Moniz
kalama ala 
pi tenpo suno kama laso walo 
la waso loje pi kalama suli li weka. 
len walo pi tenpo lete li lape lon ma; 
ona li toki e awen kama pi tenpo seli 
la mi ken lape lon.

[In the silence 
Of the pale blue tomorrow.
The noisy red bird is gone.
The white robe of winter sleeps on the land.
If it bespeaks the constant coming of summer,
I can sleep soundly.]
