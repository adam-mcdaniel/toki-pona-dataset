  John Clifford ni li tawa pona tawa ni: sina kama jo e pu. taso sina toki ala e wile sona. sina toki e ni taso: "mi sona ala e nasin ni: mi toki e nimi Inli "maybe" kepeken toki pona". Every predicate, whether noun verb, adjective or preposition, begins with 'li' (except after 'mi', sina' and 'o' standing alone), so 'tawa' need 'li' too. "Forget" is indeed usually 'tawa weka tan lawa'.
