[Four maxims of the way of the Buddha

Greetings!  For four months I have been studying toki pona.  Yesterday, I did this,

Existence is suffering.
Suffering has a cause.
A person can get rid of suffering.
There is a way to remove suffering.

If you understand these word, write this in English.
I preserve this. 
Goodbye.]
