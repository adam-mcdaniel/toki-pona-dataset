Anna Fedorova

Joshua Birns-Sprague

Anne Campbell 
kin, taso "tenpo esun" li seme?

Joshua Birns-Sprague 
tenpo esun li jo e tenpo suno tu tu tu wan.

Serge Ganachaud 
tenpo esun li pona lili tawa ni. tenpo mun li pona mute. pini pi tenpo mun wan la, sina ken lukin e ni: kule pi kasi suli li ante mute.

Joshua Birns-Sprague 
tawa mi la kasi li kule mute lon tenpo esun tu wan. tenpo ni li pini la kasi li pimeja li jo e lipu kasi ala.

Anne Campbell 
tan seme?

Joshua Birns-Sprague 
tenpo pi kasi kule li pona mute tawa mi. taso ona li tenpo lili lon ma pi mi en sina. ken la sona mi li pakala. mi wile e tenpo suli pi kasi kule.a, tenpo ni li pona tan ni: kon li lete lili. :)

Anne Campbell 
mi sona e ni. taso, mi sona ala e ni: nimi "tenpo esun" li sama "tenpo suno tu tu tu wan" tan seme?

Serge Ganachaud
 tan ni: nimi "tenpo esun" li wile toki e tenpo pali en musi. sina pali lon tomo esun la, sina pali lon tenpo suno tu tu tu li musi lon tenpo suno wan.

Anne Campbell

Joshua Birns-Sprague 
Market time consists of seven days.
Serge Ganachaud 
Market time is of little use for this.  A month is very useful.  At the end of one month, you can see the colors of the tree have changed a lot.
