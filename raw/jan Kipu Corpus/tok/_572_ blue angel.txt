Marcus Miles
tenpo pi pali ala la mi en jan olin mi li tawa e ma kasi. ma kasi li lon poka telo suli. mi lukin e tomo tawa kon. tomo tawa kon li jo e nimi. ona li tomo tawa kon Pulu Ankele. ona li moku e suwi e telo nasa.

Anne Campbell 
nimi "Pulu Ankele" li seme? mi mute ken ala ken lukin e sitelen?

Marcus Miles 
tenpo lili lili la mi pali e sitelen. tomo tawa kon li tomo tawa kon Jan pi Toki Sewi pi Kule Laso.

John Clifford 
ken la 'sina tu li tawa ma kasi' (sina jo ala e ma kasi tawa ma ante.) mi lukin e 'tomo tawa kon 'Pulu Ankele' sama nimi Inli "Blue Angels" taso mi sona ala e nimi 'Jan pi Toki Sewi' sama nimi Inli "Angel".
Anne Campbell 
a! mi sona e Jan pi Toki Sewi pi Kule Laso!



Anne Campbell 
tenpo pini, la mije olin en mi li kama wan. mi tu li lon ma tomo San Pansico. Jan pi Toki Sewi pi Kule Laso li lon kin. mije en mi li toki e ni: "mi ken olin e sina lon tenpo ali." lon sewi, la tomo tawa kon laso li toki e ni: "PIIIIIIUUUUUUUUNN."


Piotr Mittelstaedt 
Anne Campbell o, pona mute tawa sina tu!


Piotr Mittelstaedt

[Marcus Miles
During vacation, my wife and I went to a park.  The park was on the ocean beach. We saw planes.  These planes had a name.  They were the Pulu Ankele planes.  They ate sweets and alcohol.
Anne Campbell
What is `Pulu Ankele'?  Can we read this text? 

Marcus Miles
I wrote this very rapidly. The planes are the planes People who Speak God of the Color Blue. 

John Clifford 
I read "planes of  Pulu Ankele' as the same as "Blue Angels".  But I don't understand "People who Speak God" as "Angel".
Anne Campbell 
Oh, I know the Blue Angels.



Anne Campbell 
My husband and I got married.  We were in San Francisco. The blue Angels also were there.  Husband and I said "I can love you forever."  in the sky, the blue planes said "Boom!"



Piotr Mittelstaedt 
Anne Campbell, the best to you two!!


Piotr Mittelstaedt 
To me, the expression "People who Speak God" is clear without difficulty.  If the holy spirit sends the speech of God, it is the spirit who speaks God. If this spirit has a form, he is an angel.]
