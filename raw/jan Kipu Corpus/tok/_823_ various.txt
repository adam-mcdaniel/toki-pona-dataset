Daraffa
tenpo ni la jan li pilin ike lon ma Mewika. mute mani  li lili.  jan mute li jo
ala e pali awen. tan wan li ni: jan li jo ala e mani. jan mute li wile jo e
tomo. kulupu lawa li toko e ni: tomo mani li wile pana e mani tawa jan. jan li
pana e mani tawa tomo. taso jan li ken ala pana e mani tawa tomo. tomo mani li
kama jo e tomo jan. tomo mani li pini pana e mani. tomo mani li pini.  pona pi lipu esun li anpa. lawa esun li kama jo e mani mute. ni li pona ala tawa mi.
jan li ken ala  pana e mani tawa tomo tawa. esun pi tomo tawa li pini.  
kulupu lawa li kama jo e tomo mani e esun pi tomo tawa. ni li nasin ala pi ma
Mewika.

 Daraffa
tenpo mun pini la jan  Mikeli Sakison [Michael Jackson] li moli. ona li jan pi tawa musi pona li
jan pi kalama musi pona. kama kulupu ona li lon ma tomo Ele. sijelo ona li
insa e poki pi kiwen jelo. jan suli mute li toki. mije sama pi jan Mikeli li
kalama musi. jan mute li pilin ike. jan lili tu wan pi jan Mikeli li pilin ike.
jan pona pi Mikeli li len e len luka wan. jan utala pona li wile sona e ni: jan
Mikeli li moli tan seme?

Daraffa
anpa sinpin mi li suli. mi wile e ni: mi jo e anpa sinpin lili. mi moku e pan
lili e kili mute. mi moku ala e suwi. mi moku e moku pona. mi pali e sijelo mi.
mi sewi li anpa e ijo suli. ijo li jo e nimi "Kettlebells". mi wile e ni: sijelo
mi li kama wawa. pali musi sijelo pi kepeken ijo "Kettlebells" li pona tawa mi. tenpo
pini la mi ken pali e musi sijelo lon tenpo lili. tenpo ni la mi ken pali e musi
sijelo lon tenpo suli. kin la mi tawa noka lon nasin lon tenpo suli.

Alice didn't like this.  She was on the bank of  a brook beside her sister and doing nothing.  Her sister was reading a book.  Occasionally, Alice peeked at it.  It didn't have pictures.
 [opening of Alice] 

Daraffa
Now people feel bad in America.   The quantity of money is small.  Many people don't have a permanent job.  One cause is that people don't have money.  Many people want to have a home.  
Lawmakers say that banks ought to give money to people.  People will give money for homes.  But people can't give money for homes.  Banks get the people's homes.  The banks stop giving money.  The banks fail.  The value of stocks is down.  Business leaders get a lot of money.  This is not good for me.  People cannot give money for cars.  Car dealerships close.  The government gets the banks and the car dealerships.  This is not the American way.  

 Daraffa
Last month, Michael Jackson died.  He was a good dancer and a good singer.  His funeral was in LA.  His body was inside a gold coffin.  Many important people spoke.  Michael's brothers sag.  Many people were sad.  Michael's three children were sad.  Michael's friends wore one glove.  The police want to know why Michael died.

My belly is big.  I want to have a small belly.  I eat little grain and a lot of fruit.  I don't eat sugar.  I eat good food.  I work my body. I raise and lower big things,  these things are called "Kettlebells" and are good for me.  I used to be able to work exercises for a little while.  Now I can work exercises for a long while.  Moreover, I walk the track for a long time. ]
