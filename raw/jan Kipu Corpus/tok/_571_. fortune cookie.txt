In Chinese food, one gives a cookie.  There is a slip of paper inside the cookie. There is a writing of advice or the future on the slip.  I saw this on a slip: "If you fix something, that is a good deed; But this is better: if you talk about goodness to people, the people become strong.

  Joseph Gary Engelhardt 
  My fortune cookie slip said "A long journey starts from a short journey."
