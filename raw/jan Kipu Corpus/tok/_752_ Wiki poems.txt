telo sewi pi tenpo lete 
li lon lupa tomo mi.
tan ni, la ma kasi li laso jelo.
tan ni, la kili li suli e suli ona.
tan ni, la mi lukin ala e suno.
tan ni, la mi kama laso kin.     [`laso' not an emotion word in tp  --  yet.]
----

toki musi pi soweli lili tomo mi, tan jan Wiki
soweli lili lape o,
tenpo suno la
sina moku e seli suno.
tenpo pimeja la
sina moku e seli mi.

by jan Wiki
Winter rain
falls on the window of my home.
Because of this, the meadows are green.
Because of this, the fruit grows larger.
Because of this, I cannot see the sun.
Because of this, I am feeling sad, indeed.
