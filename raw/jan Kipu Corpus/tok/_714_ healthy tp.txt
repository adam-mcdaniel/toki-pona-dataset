tenpo sike wan pi toki pona li pini tawa mi.
mi pilin e ni: mi toki pona la mi pona e kepeken mi pi toki Inli.
toki pona li nasin ni: mi ken musi e toki insa mi lon ale. musi li pona e kon mi. kon mi li pona la pilin mi li kama pona. pilin mi li pona la sijelo mi li kama pona. tan ni la toki pona li pona e ale mi.
tenpo kama la mi awen lon toki pona.

John Clifford
 o awen.




[Joshua Birns-Sprague
One year of toki pona are finished for me.
I think that when I speak well, I improve my english usage.  
toki pona is this way: I can entertain my thought in everything.  Entertaining is good for my spirit.  If my spirit is good, my feelings become good.  If my feelings are good, my body become good.  So, toki pona improves all of me.  
I will remain in toki pona.

John Clifford
Continue.]
