'''kulupu kule''' li kulupu jan pi nasin ante. ona li jo e kulupu pi nasin olin ante anu kulupu pi nasin unpa ante e kulupu [[tonsi]]. kulupu lili ante pi kulupu kule li ni:
* [[mije pi olin mije]]
* [[meli pi olin meli]]
* [[jan pi nasin olin tu]]
* [[jan pi olin ale]]
*[[tonsi]]
* [[jan pi wile unpa ala]]
== nimi ==
nimi pi toki pona li tan ni: len kule li sitelen pi kulupu ni.
[[toki Inli]] la jan li kepeken e nimi ni: "''LGBT''". ni li lili tan '''''L'''esbian'' (meli pi olin meli), '''''G'''ay'' (mije pi olin mije), '''''B'''isexual'' (jan pi nasin olin tu), en '''''T'''ransgender'' (tonsi). tenpo mute la, ona li jo e sitelen ante, sama e LGBTQ e LGBT+ e LGBTI anu ante.
tenpo mute en ma mute la, jan li ike tawa jan kule. taso, jan kule li pali tawa kama jo pona en ken lon kulupu jan.
