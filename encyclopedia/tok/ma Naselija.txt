{{nimi ijo|'''ma Naselija'''|Nigeria|toki Inli|ipa=nadri|}} li ma lon [[ma Apika pi pini suno]]. ma Naselija li lon poka pi ma ni: [[ma Pene]] en [[ma Nise]] en [[ma Sate]] en [[ma Kamelun]]. mute jan la ma Naselija li suli nanpa wan lon ma Apika. mute jan la [[ma tomo Eko]] (ma tomo Lako) <!-- Lagos--> li suli nanpa wan lon ma Naselija. jan mute lon ma Naselija anpa li alasa e [[nasin sewi Kolisu]], taso jan mute lon ma Naselija sewi li alasa e [[nasin sewi Isilan]].
==nimi ma==
nimi lawa pi ma Naselija li {{nimi ijo|"Petela Lipupi A Naselija"|Federal Republic of Nigeria|toki Inli}}.
nimi ona li kama tan [[ma telo Nise]]. nimi pi [[ma Nise]] li kama tan nasin telo sama.
==tenpo pini==
tenpo pini la, ma Naselija li ma lawa mute:
* [[ma suli Ojo]] <!-- Oyo Empire -->
* [[ma Eto]] <!-- Kingdom of Benin -->
* [[ma Sokoto]] <!-- Sokoto -->
* [[ma Ponu]] <!-- Bornu Empire --->
* ma ante mute
taso, tenpo pi poka ona, [[ma Juke]] li lanpan e ona li wan e ma ni li kama kipisi pi [[ma lawa suli Piten]]. ma Naselija li weka tan lawa pi ma Juke lon tenpo sike nanpa 1960.
