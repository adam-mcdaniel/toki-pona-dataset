{{nimi ijo|'''toki Nosiki'''|Norsk|toki Nosiki||ipa=}} li toki lawa pi [[ma Nosiki]]. ona li lon [[kulupu toki Kemani]]. toki Nosiki en [[toki Wensa]] en [[toki Tansi]] li tan toki mama sama.
==nasin sitelen==
nasin sitelen tu li lon tawa toki Nosiki. jan ala li toki e nasin ni. taso, jan ale li ken kepeken ona.
===toki lipu===
{{nimi ijo|toki lipu|Bokml|toki Nosiki}} la, jan li kepeken ona tan ni: tenpo pini la, [[ma Tansi]] li lawa e ma Nosiki. ma Tansi li kama lawa ala e ona la, ma Nosiki li awen kepeken [[toki Tansi]] tawa lipu. taso, ma Nosiki li ante lili e ona. jan mute pi ma Nosiki li sitelen e toki ni. toki lipu en toki Tansi la, ona li ante lili:
*nimi pi toki Tansi li kepeken kalama b/d/g la, toki lipu li kepeken kalama p/t/k. toki Tansi: ''lbe, hedde, kage''. toki lipu: ''lpe, hete, kake''.
*nimi jan li ante lili. toki Tansi: ''I - jer - jeres'' . toki lipu: ''dere - dere - deres.''
===toki Ninosiki===
{{nimi ijo|toki Ninosiki|Nynorsk|toki Nosiki}} la, [[jan Iwa Osen]] li pali e ona. ona li wile pali e nasin sitelen pi toki Nosiki tan ni: toki Tansi li ante mute tawa jan pi ma Nosiki. ona li tawa ale lon ma Nosiki la, ona li kute e nasin toki pi jan ale. toki lipu en toki Ninosiki li sama lili. taso, ona tu li ante:
*kalama li ante. toki lipu: ''hete, lse, grt''. toki Ninosiki: ''heita, lysa, graut''.
*nimi pi toki lipu kepeken kalama ''be-, an-, -het, -else'' la, toki Nosiki li kepeken kalama ni ala. o lukin e nimi ni. toki lipu: ''beskjed, beskrive, anbefale, ansette, trygghet, anelse''. toki Ninosiki: ''melding, skildra, tilrda, tilsetja, tryggje, aning''.
[[sitelen:Uthuslien Aasen 1891.jpg|thumb|sitelen pi jan Iwa Osen.]]
toki Ninosiki en nasin toki pi jan mute pi ma Nosiki li sama mute. kin la, jan mute li kepeken toki lipu. jan pi mute lili taso li kepeken toki Ninosiki.
==nasin toki==
ma mute li weka e nasin toki. taso, ma Nosiki li awen e nasin toki. jan Nosiki mute li kepeken nasin toki ona. jan mute pilin e ni: ona li pilin pona tan nasin toki ona. ni li kulupu pi nasin toki:
*{{nimi ijo|toki Nosiki anpa|stnorsk/Austnorsk|4=|ipa=}}
*{{nimi ijo|toki Nosiki poka|Vestnorsk|4=|ipa=}}
*{{nimi ijo|toki Nosiki insa|Trndersk|4=|ipa=}}
*{{nimi ijo|toki Nosiki sewi|Nordnorsk|4=|ipa=}}
jan Nosiki ale ken sona e nasin toki ante. taso, tenpo lili la, jan li ken sona ale ala e nasin toki.
