{{nimi ijo|'''jan Ape Sinso'''| |toki Nijon}} li jan pi [[nasin lawa]] tan [[ma Nijon]]. ona li kama jan lawa pi ma Nijon li kama jan lawa pi [[kulupu Siminto]] lon tenpo sike nanpa 2006 li pini e lawa ona lon tenpo sike nanpa 2007. tenpo nanpa tu la, ona li kama jan lawa lon tenpo sike nanpa 2012, li pini e lawa lon tenpo sike nanpa 2020. tenpo sike nanpa 2022 la, ona li moli tan ni: jan li moli ona kepeken [[ilo utala]].
[[kulupu mama]] pi jan Ape Sinso li suli lon nasin lawa pi ma Nijon. mama mije pi mama meli ona, {{nimi ijo|[[jan Kisi Nopusuke]]| , Kishi Nobusuke}} li jan lawa pi tenpo pini. jan Ape Sinso li alasa e [[nasin pi wawa ma|nasin ma]] e [[nasin awen]]. ona li toki e ni: ijo ike pi pali tan ma Nijon lon tenpo pini li kama ala.<ref>[https://www.newyorker.com/news/q-and-a/how-shinzo-abe-sought-to-rewrite-japanese-history How Shinzo Abe Sought to Rewrite Japanese History] ''The New Yorker''</ref> ona li wile tawa ante e lipu lawa nanpa wan pi ma Nijon tan ni: ona li weka e ken pi ma Nijon tawa open utala.<ref>[https://www.japantimes.co.jp/news/2016/02/03/national/politics-diplomacy/abe-explicit-call-amendment-constitutions-article-9/ Abe explicit in call for amendment to Constitution's Article 9] ''The Japan Times''</ref> ona li pali tawa suli e esun pi ma Nijon.
{{nimi ijo|jan Jamakami Tesuja|, Yamagami Tetsuya}} li moli e ona lon tenpo sike nanpa 2022 lon [[ma tomo Nala]].
