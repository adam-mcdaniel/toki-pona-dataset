{{nimi ijo|'''jan Pane Sasu'''|Bjarne Stroustrup|toki Tansi||ipa=}} li mama pi [[toki ilo Sipupu]] li jan sona pi ilo sona li jan pi pana sona lon tomo sona Texas A&M. tenpo 1950-12-30 la ona li kama lon ma tomo {{nimi ijo|Owu, Tansi|Aarhus, Danmark|toki Tansi||ipa=}}. jan li sona e ona tan ni: ona li pali e toki pi ilo sona toki pali C++.
tenpo 1979 la jan Sasu li pali e toki C++. ona li toki ni: mi pali e toki C++ li sitelen e nasin ni pi tenpo pini mute li pali e lon ni pi nanpa wan. jan Sasu li sitelen kin e lipu mute Toki Ilo Sipupu(toki Inli:The C++ Programming Language). jan mute li pilin e ni: lipu mute ni li lipu mute sewi tawa toki ni.
jan Sasu li jo e lipu cand. scient. tawa sona nanpa en sona pi ilo sona tan tomo sona lon {{nimi ijo|Owu, Tansi|Aarhus, Danmark|toki Tansi||ipa=}}. ona li jo kin e lipu Ph.D. tawa sona pi ilo sona tan tomo sona lon {{nimi ijo|Kanpi, Inli|Cambridge, England|toki Inli||ipa=}}. tenpo pini tawa tenpo 2002 la ona li jan lawa pi kama sona kulupu pi ilo sona pi kulupu AT&T Lab. tenpo 2004 la jan li ken e ni: jan Stroustrup li jan kulupu pi kulupu The National Academy of Engineering. jan Stroustrup li jan kulupu pi kulupu ACM li jan kulupu pi kulupu IEEE.
