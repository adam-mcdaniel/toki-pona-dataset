{{nimi ijo|'''musi pi sike palisa tan ma Mewika'''|American Football|toki Inli||ipa=}} li [[musi sijelo]] li [[musi sike]]. mute la jan li musi e ona lon [[ma Mewika]]. ona li musi sijelo nanpa wan tawa kulupu jan pi ma ni. musi sama la, musi ni li ante mute li suli mute. jan pi musi ni li anpa e ona li utala suli la musi sike ante li utala lili.
toki Inli pi ma Mewika la, nimi pi musi ni li ni: "musi pi sike noka". [[ma Juke]] la, toki Inli la, nimi pi musi ni li ni: "musi pi sike noka tan ma Mewika", tan nimi pi ma Juke li musi ante pi sike noka. [[ma Oselija]] la, "musi pi sike noka" li musi pi sike palisa tan ma Oselija. [[ma Kanata]] la "musi pi sike noka" li musi pi sike palisa pi ma Kanata. [[ma Alan]] la, "musi pi sike noka" li musi sijelo sike tan ma Alan.
jan pi musi ni li lon nasin mute. jan ken musi pona e musi ni, ona li lon kulupu nanpa wan pi musi pi sike palisa pi ma Mewika (toki Inli: National Football League, NFL). kulupu mute luka luka tu pi musi ni li lon. kulupu pi musi ni li lon tomo sona kin.
