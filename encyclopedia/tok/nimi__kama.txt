* nimi '''kama''' li toki e ijo mute la, sina ken toki e kon lawa ona lon linja ni.
# ''nimi ijo'': pali suli pi ijo mute.
#: ''kama ni li pona.''
# ''nimi ante'': tenpo ni, ijo ni li lon ala. taso, ona li ken lon tenpo kama.
#: ''kulupu suli li kama.''
