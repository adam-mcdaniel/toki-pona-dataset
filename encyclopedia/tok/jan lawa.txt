'''jan lawa''' li lawa e [[kulupu jan]]. jan lawa li toki tawa jan mute. jan lawa o pali e ijo suli mute.
[[lawa ma]] li jo e jan lawa. nasin ante la jan li kama jan lawa. [[jan So Piten]] li jan lawa pi [[ma Mewika]]. [[nasin wile pi jan lawa]] la jan So Piten li kama jan lawa. [[jan lawa Elisepe nanpa tu]] li jan lawa pi [[ma Juke]]. mama pi jan Elisapesi nanpa tu li jan lawa li moli la jan Elisapesi nanpa tu li kama jan lawa. ma ante la, jan li [[lanpan lawa|lanpan e lawa]]. [[jan Penito Musolini]] li lanpan e lawa pi [[ma Italija]] lon tenpo sike 1922.
kulupu ante kin li jo e jan lawa. [[jan Ilon Mu]] li jan lawa pi [[kulupu Tesa]].
lawa ma li ken jo e jan lawa mute. [[jan lawa Elisipe nanpa tu]] li [[jan lawa mama]] pi ma Juke. [[jan Powi Sonsen]] li [[jan nanpa wan pi lawa ma]]. tenpo pini la, jan lawa mama li toki wile e jan nanpa wan pi lawa ma lon ma Juke. tenpo sin la, [[kulupu lawa]] anpa pi ma Juke li toki wile e jan nanpa wan pi lawa ma.
jan lawa li ike la, jan li ken weka e ona. [[utala]] anu [[utala ala]] la jan li ken weka e jan lawa. [[nasin wile pi jan lawa]] la, jan ale li toki e ni: jan seme o kama jan lawa. jan li jo e wile pi mute nanpa wan la, jan li kama lawa.
==jan lawa mama==
[[sitelen:Queen_Elizabeth_II_in_March_2015.jpg|thumb|150px|[[jan lawa Elisepe nanpa tu]] li jan lawa sewi pi [[ma Juke]]]]
jan lawa mama li lawa lon tenpo ale ona. ken la, ona li [[jan lawa sewi pi lipu lawa]] li [[jan lawa sewi pi wawa ale]].
jan lawa sewi li ken lawa e ma mute. [[jan Alisapa nanpa tu]] li lawa e [[ma Juke]] e [[ma Kanata]] e ma ante.
tenpo mute la, ona li pana e wawa tawa [[jan lili]] lon ona li moli.
tenpo pini la, [[lawa ma]] mute mute li jo e jan lawa sewi.
