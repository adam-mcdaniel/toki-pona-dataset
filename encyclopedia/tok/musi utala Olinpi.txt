{{nimi ijo|'''musi utala Olinpi'''|Olympics}} li musi utala suli mute pi [[musi sijelo]]. suli la, ona li musi utala nanpa wan pi musi sijelo lon ma ali. jan pi musi sijelo tan ma mute li utala musi e jan ante. jan li lon kulupu pona pi jan tan ma sama ona. musi sijelo mute li lon musi utala Olinpi. musi utala Olinpo ante li lon [[tenpo seli]] li lon [[tenpo lete]]. tenpo sike nanpa tu tu li pini la, musi utala ni li kama.
tenpo pini la, jan lon [[ma Elena majuna]] li open e [[musi utala Olinpo pi tenpo pini]]. ni li lon [[ma tomo Olinpija]] lon ma Elena. musi utala Olinpi sin li kama lon tenpo sike 1896. kulupu IOC li kama li pali e musi utala lon [[ma tomo Asina]]. tenpo ni la, ma pi musi Olinpi ante li lon [[ma tomo]] mute ante.
