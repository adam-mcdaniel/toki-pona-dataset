'''sitelen tawa''' li awen e ijo pali. ona li ken jo e [[kalama]]. ken la, ona li pana lukin pi [[sitelen lon|ijo lon]] anu [[sitelen tawa pi lon ala|ijo pi lon ala]]. sitelen tawa li jo e sitelen mute pi tawa ala. sitelen ni li pana lon poka pi sitelen ante la, ona li lukin sama e tawa. sitelen tawa li pona li [[musi]] tawa [[jan]] mute.
sitelen tawa pi tenpo ni li kama lon tenpo sike luka luka 1890s. taso, jan li jo e nasin ante pi pana e lukin sama tawa lon sitelen lon tenpo pini.  sitelen tawa li kama suli lon [[nasin jan]]. tenpo sin la, sitelen tawa li pona tawa jan pi ma ali. jan li ken lukin e sitelen tawa kepeken [[tomo pi sitelen tawa]] anu [[ilo pi sitelen weka]] anu [[ilo sona]].
tenpo open la, pimeja en walo li [[kule]] taso lon sitelen tawa. taso tenpo ni la, sitelen tawa ken jo e kule ali.
