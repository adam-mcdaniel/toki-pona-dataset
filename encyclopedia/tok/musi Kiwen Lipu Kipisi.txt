==nasin musi==
jan musi li toki e wan pi nimi musi lon tenpo sama. tenpo pi wan nanpa tu wan la, ona li pana e sijelo lon tenpo sama kepeken luka ona. sijelo ni li ken:
* [[sitelen:SssStein.jpg|32px]] '''kiwen''' - palisa luka ale li lon insa luka
* [[sitelen:SssPapier.jpg|32px]] '''lipu''' - palisa ale li lon ala insa luka, li lon poka pi ona sama
* [[sitelen:SssSchere.jpg|32px]] (ilo) '''kipisi''' - palisa pi suli poka li nanpa wan la, palisa nanpa tu en palisa nanpa tu wan taso li lon ala insa luka
sijelo li sama la, jan ala li sewi; jan tu o musi sin. sijelo li ante la, ona li utala kepeken nasin ni:
* lipu li len e kiwen (li sewi tawa kiwen)
* ilo kipisi li kipisi e lipu
* kiwen li pakala e ilo kipisi
jan pi sijelo sewi li sewi, anu li kama jo e nanpa pona.
sijelo ale li sewi tawa sijelo wan, li anpa tawa sijelo ante. ni la, sijelo ala li pona nanpa wan.
==sona namako==
* nasin utala ni li lon musi ante kin. [[musi Pokemon]] la, wawa seli li pakala pona e soweli kasi, taso wawa kasi li pakala pona e soweli telo, taso wawa telo li pakala pona e soweli seli.
