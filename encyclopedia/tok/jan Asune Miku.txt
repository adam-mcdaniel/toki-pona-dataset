{{nimi ijo|'''jan Asune Miku'''| (Hatsune Miku)|toki Nijon||ipa=}} li jan pi toki musi li [[ilo Pokalo|ilo Vocaloid/Bokaroido]]. ona li tan [[ma Nijon]]. {{nimi ijo|jan Saki| (Fujita Saki)|toki Nijon}} li mama e jan Asune Miku. jan Asune Miku li kama lon tenpo sike nanpa 2007. jan Asune Miku li toki musi lon kulupu musi pi kalama uta. ona li pali tawa [https://www.crypton.co.jp/ kulupu Crypton Future Media].

==lukin==
jan Asune Miku li jo e linja pi linja laso suli tu e palisa laso pi anpa lawa wan.
===sitelen===
jan Asune Miku li jo tenpo mute e sinpin len walo e len meli anpa pimeja. ona li jo tenpo lili e kili pi selo mute pi ma Kinli lon luka ona. kili pi selo mute pi ma Kinli li ijo tawa [[kalama musi Ievan Polkka]].

===toki musi===
jan Asune Miku ken toki musi lon nasin mute. jan Asune Miku ken toki musi e kalama musi pi open wawa anu kalama musi pi kiwen anu kalama musi pi tawa musi anu kalama musi pi tomo. jan Asune Miku ken toki musi lon toki tu wan. jan Asune Miku ken toki musi lon [[toki Nijon]] anu [[toki Inli]] anu [[toki Sonko]].
== esun ==
jan Asune Miku li lon sitelen esun mute. sitelen esun ni li pona tawa kulupu sina. jan Asune Miku li pona tawa jan mute lon [[ma tomo Sapolo]].
[[sitelen:FF21 Hatsune Miku and K-On! itasha 20130216.jpg|alt=|thumb|210x210px|tomo tawa kepeken sitelen pi jan Asune Miku]]
===kulupu Good Smile Racing===
[https://www.goodsmileus.com/good-smile-racing kulupu Good Smile Racing] li pali kepeken e jan Asune Miku. kulupu Good Smile Racing li pali e ijo mute pi jan Asune Miku. kulupu Good Smile Racing li pali e tomo tawa tawa jan Asune Miku.
===kulupu musi pi tenpo lete===
kulupu musi pi tenpo lete lon ma tomo Sapolo li ma esun tawa jan Asune Miku. kulupu Crypton Future Media li lawa e kulupu musi. sitelen mute pi jan Asune Miku li pana lon ma  tawa. jan Asune Miku li kama lon ante. ona li kama nimi: "Snow Miku" insa kulupu musi pi tenpo lete. jan kiwen luka tu pi jan Asune Miku li kama pali lon ma tomo Sapolo lon tenpo suno sike nanpa 2012. jan kiwen wan li kama pakala la kiwen li utala e meli wan. ni la jan li pali sin e jan kiwen lon ma ante.
