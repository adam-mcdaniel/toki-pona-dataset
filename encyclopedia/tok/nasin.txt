ijo li tawa lon '''nasin'''. nasin ante mute li lon. nasin li sama linja. nasin pi lili nanpa wan lon insa pi ijo tu li sama palisa.
==nasin lon ma==
nasin ala la, jan li ken ala tawa.
[[jan]] en [[ilo tawa]] li tawa lon nasin. jan li pali e nasin mute. nasin mute li kama wan la, [[wan nasin]] li lon. wan nasin la, jan li ken tawa lon nasin ante. mute la, nasin ala la [[tomo tawa]] mute li ken ala tawa li ken kama pakala. nasin li ken jo e selo kiwen. nasin li jo ala e selo kiwen la, jan li ken jo ala e mani mute.
===nasin lon sewi===
jan li ken ala tawa lon sewi ijo la jan li pali e nasin lon sewi ijo. nasin mute li lon sewi [[nasin telo]]. jan li ken pali e nasin lon sewi nasin ante.
nasin lon sewi li kepeken e [[sijelo]] ante e nasin [[tomo]] ante. nasin mute li kepeken e [[sike]]. nasin suli lon sewi li ken kepeken e [[linja]] mute. linja suli tu li linja e tomo suli tu. linja lili mute lon anpa linja suli tu li linja e nasin.
==nasin telo==
{{lipu suli|nasin telo}}
[[telo]] li tawa la, nasin telo li lon. nasin telo li suli la, jan li ken tawa kepeken [[tomo tawa telo]] lon nasin telo.

ijo li tawa la, ona li pana e nasin tawa. jan en [[ilo sona]] li ken kama sona e nasin pi nasin tawa kepeken [[pali nanpa]]
